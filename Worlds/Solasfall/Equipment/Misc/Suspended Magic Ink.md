A specialised magical ink that defies gravity when applied. The ink contains a suspension of fine mana-reactive particles in a stabilising agent that, when exposed to air, creates a rigid but brittle structure that holds its position in space.

**Properties:**
- Circles drawn with Suspended Ink float in mid-air at the position they were drawn
- Can be disrupted by:
  - Strong winds (GM discretion, typically winds above 30km/h)
  - Physical contact with the drawn lines (breaks the structure)

- Due to its structure and properties, weave casted spells can be formed in 3 Dimensions, this creates a more denser and more complex spell. All Drawing Time is increased by +3 but gain 10 free enhancement points for the spell, representing it gets better with added complexity.
