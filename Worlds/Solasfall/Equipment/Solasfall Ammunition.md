Ammunition is consumed on use and must be restocked between expeditions. Each piece of ammunition is **Weightless** individually. Every 20 of the same ammunition type counts as 1 capacity unit. See [[Carrying Limits]] for bundling rules.

Ammunition has no inherent [[Damage Types|damage]] properties — [[Damage Types|damage]] is determined entirely by the [[Weapon|weapon]] firing it.

### Standard Arrows

**Weight Class:** Weightless
**Quantity:** Sold in bundles of 20.

### Heavy Arrows
