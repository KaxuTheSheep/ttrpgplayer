Equipment represents the tools, protection, and gear a character carries into combat or exploration.  
It influences how they **[[Attacks|attack]]**, **defend**, **move**, and **endure** danger.
Equipment never changes a character’s **[[Attributes]]** directly — instead, it affects:
- [[Sturdiness]] (how long you endure physical harm)
- [[Blood]] loss potential (via reducing incoming [[Damage Types|damage]])
- [[Evasion]] (how easily you are struck)
- [[Movement Types|Movement]] Types (how far you can reposition each [[Rounds|round]])
- [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] for certain [[Actions]] (e.g., casting, aiming, reacting)
## Categories of Equipment
- **[[Armour]]**  
  Protects the body by reducing incoming [[Damage Types|damage]] (**DR**).  
  Heavier [[Armour]] requires higher **[[Attributes|Might]]** to wear effectively and reduces mobility.
- **[[Shields]]**  
  Defensive tools used with the **[[Block]]** [[Actions|action]].  
  [[Shields]] enhance reactive defense rather than passive [[Evasion]].
- **[[Weapons]]** 
  [[Weapons]] differ by **[[Damage Types|damage]]**, **[[Speed & Resolution|Speed]]**, and later **Maneuver traits**.
