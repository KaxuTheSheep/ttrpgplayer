# Status
*Feared, tolerated, and respected for strength.*
Created by a [[Hollowdeep]] parasite that fundamentally alters the host's [[Mana|mana]] signature, transforming them into something new. Marked by pale skin, shark-like teeth, pupil-less eyes, and long tongues — often mistaken for demons. Tolerated only for their martial usefulness and ability to resonate with [[Rift]] energy. Most faithful distrust them, though they are not barred from participating in religion. Their transformations are taboo within city walls, even when offered as a form of rebirth to the old or dying.
# Culture
Velkhari form no more than three meaningful bonds; casual associations are common but trust is rare. The transformation overwrites the old self entirely — no memories or feelings of their past life remain. The [[Mana|mana]] signature that defined who they were is replaced by something new, something shaped by the parasite's influence.

In their own district they thrive in a competitive sparring culture, maintaining a loose but respected hierarchy based on [[Skills|skill]] and combat prowess. Their perspective on the [[Hollowdeep]] varies: some see it as home, others as a place of growth or challenge.
# Relationships with Other Races
Widely mistrusted, especially by Humans and the faithful. Generally tolerated due to their usefulness and fearsome reputation — people avoid provoking them. Drenek and Murks may respect their power but keep a wide emotional distance. Velkhari treat others case-by-case, forming opinions based on individual conduct rather than race.
# Appearance
Pale, almost ghostly skin with shark-like teeth and long tongues. Pupil-less eyes that reflect [[Rift]] energy. Typically wear minimal, practical clothing suited for combat or climbing. Their bodies often show faint [[Rift]] markings or scars from transformation.

# Traits
### Predator's Scent
Your parasite-enhanced physiology grants you a heightened sense of smell. You can detect creatures within 5m by scent alone, even through walls or in total darkness. Heavily obscured or odourless creatures may reduce this [[Range & Threat|range]] at GM discretion.
### Predator's Bite
As an [[Attacks|attack]], you may bite a target within melee [[Range & Threat|range]], dealing 1d4 + [[Attributes|Might]] Piercing [[Damage Types|damage]] at [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] 12 + [[Attributes|Grace]].

## Spider Climb
Prerequisites: None.
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
You can crawl on vertical surfaces. [[Movement Types|Movement]] on walls uses your Crawl Travel Rate.

## Wall Ceiling
Prerequisites: Spider Climb. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your grip extends to inverted surfaces. You can crawl on ceilings as well as walls.

## [[Rift]] Step
Prerequisites: None.
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Harnessing your connection to [[Rift]] energy, you tear open a momentary portal within line of sight. As a Half [[Actions|Action]] at [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] 10, teleport up to 6m. Once per Short Rest.

## [[Rift]] Reach
Prerequisites: [[Rift]] Step. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your [[Rift]] Step [[Range & Threat|range]] increases to 9m.

## Blind Step
Prerequisites: [[Rift]] Step. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
You no longer require line of sight to use [[Rift]] Step. You must have previously seen or been to the destination, or have a precise sensory fix on it — scent, sound, or similar.

## [[Rift]] Surge
Prerequisites: [[Rift]] Reach, Blind Step. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
[[Rift]] Step recharges on any Rest rather than Short Rest only. You may now use it once per any Rest.

## Enhanced [[Rift]] Surge
Prerequisites: [[Rift]] Surge.
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
[[Rift]] Step now gains multiple charges. You may now use it twice per Rest.

## Keen Scent
Prerequisites: None. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your parasite-enhanced scent sharpens further. Predator's Scent [[Range & Threat|range]] increases from 5m to 9m.

## Scent Awareness
Prerequisites: Keen Scent. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
You cannot be surprised by creatures within your Predator's Scent [[Range & Threat|range]]. When combat begins, you have Advantage on all Tests during the first [[Rounds|round]] against any creature you detected by scent before the encounter started.

## Enhanced Predator's Bite 
Prerequisites: None. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your bite sharpens. [[Damage Types|Damage]] increases to 1d6 + [[Attributes|Might]] Piercing.

## Latch
Prerequisites: Enhanced Predator's Bite, Exclusive to [[Soul]] Taste.
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
When your bite [[Attacks|attack]] hits, you may choose to latch onto the target. The target becomes Grappled until they break free or you release. While latched, you deal 1d4 Piercing [[Damage Types|damage]] automatically at the start of each [[Rounds|round]] without spending an [[Actions|action]].

## Feeding
Prerequisites: Enhanced Predator's Bite. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
You now heal a number of blood equal to half of any [[Damage Types|damage]] dealt by your bite, this includes latch. 

## Death Grip
Prerequisites: Latch. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
While latched, the target takes a −2 penalty to [[Attributes|Might]] and [[Attributes|Grace]] Tests to break free. Additionally the automatic [[Damage Types|damage]] increases to 1d6.

## [[Soul]] Taste
Prerequisites: Enhanced Predator's Bite, Exclusive to Latch. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
When your bite [[Attacks|attack]] hits, the parasite reads the target's [[Mana|mana]] signature. Roll 2d8 and consult the [[Soul]] Taste table, gaining that information immediately.

|d8|Information|
|---|---|
|1|Rough Blood state (percentage bracket)|
|2|Whether they are under magical influence|
|3|[[Attributes|Presence]] of an Essence|
|4|Whether their [[Soul|soul]] is connected to a deity or if they are a Follower|
|5|Whether they are [[Mana|mana]] sensitive|
|6|[[Attributes|Resolve]] strength — how anchored their [[Soul|soul]] is|
|7|Dominant [[Attributes|Attribute]]|
|8|Whether they are concealing their true nature|

Duplicate rolls reveal nothing additional.

## Refined Palate
Prerequisites: [[Soul]] Taste, Exclusive to Voracious Reading. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Your parasite's reading sharpens. You now roll 1d8 instead of 2d8 — but you choose which result you receive rather than rolling randomly.

## Voracious Reading
Prerequisites: [[Soul]] Taste, Exclusive to Refined Palate. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Your parasite drinks deeper from the target's signature. Roll 3d8 instead of 2d8. Duplicate rolls reveal nothing additional.
