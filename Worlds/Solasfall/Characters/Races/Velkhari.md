# Status
*Feared, tolerated, and respected for strength.*
Created by a Hollowdeep parasite that fundamentally alters the host's mana signature, transforming them into something new. Marked by pale skin, shark-like teeth, pupil-less eyes, and long tongues — often mistaken for demons. Tolerated only for their martial usefulness and ability to resonate with Rift energy. Most faithful distrust them, though they are not barred from participating in religion. Their transformations are taboo within city walls, even when offered as a form of rebirth to the old or dying.
# Culture
Velkhari form no more than three meaningful bonds; casual associations are common but trust is rare. The transformation overwrites the old self entirely — no memories or feelings of their past life remain. The mana signature that defined who they were is replaced by something new, something shaped by the parasite's influence.

In their own district they thrive in a competitive sparring culture, maintaining a loose but respected hierarchy based on skill and combat prowess. Their perspective on the Hollowdeep varies: some see it as home, others as a place of growth or challenge.
# Relationships with Other Races
Widely mistrusted, especially by Humans and the faithful. Generally tolerated due to their usefulness and fearsome reputation — people avoid provoking them. Drenek and Murks may respect their power but keep a wide emotional distance. Velkhari treat others case-by-case, forming opinions based on individual conduct rather than race.
# Appearance
Pale, almost ghostly skin with shark-like teeth and long tongues. Pupil-less eyes that reflect Rift energy. Typically wear minimal, practical clothing suited for combat or climbing. Their bodies often show faint Rift markings or scars from transformation.
# Stats
**Base Blood:** 8
**Movement:** Deviates from standard — see below. 
	**Walk Travel Rate:** 3.5m per Speed 
	**Run Travel Rate:** 9.5m per Speed
	 **Crawl Travel Rate:** 2.5m per Speed 
	**Swim Travel Rate:** 0.5m per Speed 
	**Jump:** 4m base distance + Might (Height 1m base)
# Languages
Common and Veilscent.
# Traits
### Predator's Scent
Your parasite-enhanced physiology grants you a heightened sense of smell. You can detect creatures within 5m by scent alone, even through walls or in total darkness. Heavily obscured or odourless creatures may reduce this range at GM discretion.
### Predator's Bite
As an attack, you may bite a target within melee range, dealing 1d4 + Might Piercing damage at Speed 12 + Grace.
# Racial Web

## Spider Climb
Prerequisites: None.
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
You can crawl on vertical surfaces. Movement on walls uses your Crawl Travel Rate.

## Wall Ceiling
Prerequisites: Spider Climb. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your grip extends to inverted surfaces. You can crawl on ceilings as well as walls.

## Rift Step
Prerequisites: None.
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Harnessing your connection to Rift energy, you tear open a momentary portal within line of sight. As a Half Action at Speed 10, teleport up to 6m. Once per Short Rest.

## Rift Reach
Prerequisites: Rift Step. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your Rift Step range increases to 9m.

## Blind Step
Prerequisites: Rift Step. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
You no longer require line of sight to use Rift Step. You must have previously seen or been to the destination, or have a precise sensory fix on it — scent, sound, or similar.

## Rift Surge
Prerequisites: Rift Reach, Blind Step. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Rift Step recharges on any Rest rather than Short Rest only. You may now use it once per any Rest.

## Enhanced Rift Surge
Prerequisites: Rift Surge.
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Rift Step now gains multiple charges. You may now use it twice per Rest.

## Keen Scent
Prerequisites: None. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your parasite-enhanced scent sharpens further. Predator's Scent range increases from 5m to 9m.

## Scent Awareness
Prerequisites: Keen Scent. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
You cannot be surprised by creatures within your Predator's Scent range. When combat begins, you have Advantage on all Tests during the first round against any creature you detected by scent before the encounter started.

## Enhanced Predator's Bite 
Prerequisites: None. 
Cost: 2 Trait Points. 
Your bite sharpens. Damage increases to 1d6 + Might Piercing.

## Latch
Prerequisites: Enhanced Predator's Bite, Exclusive to Soul Taste.
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
When your bite attack hits, you may choose to latch onto the target. The target becomes Grappled until they break free or you release. While latched, you deal 1d4 Piercing damage automatically at the start of each round without spending an action.

## Feeding
Prerequisites: Enhanced Predator's Bite. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
You now heal a number of blood equal to half of any damage dealt by your bite, this includes latch. 

## Death Grip
Prerequisites: Latch. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
While latched, the target takes a −2 penalty to Might and Grace Tests to break free. Additionally the automatic damage increases to 1d6.

## Soul Taste
Prerequisites: Enhanced Predator's Bite, Exclusive to Latch. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
When your bite attack hits, the parasite reads the target's mana signature. Roll 2d8 and consult the Soul Taste table, gaining that information immediately.

|d8|Information|
|---|---|
|1|Rough Blood state (percentage bracket)|
|2|Whether they are under magical influence|
|3|Presence of an Essence|
|4|Whether their soul is connected to a deity or if they are a Follower|
|5|Whether they are mana sensitive|
|6|Resolve strength — how anchored their soul is|
|7|Dominant Attribute|
|8|Whether they are concealing their true nature|

Duplicate rolls reveal nothing additional.

## Refined Palate
Prerequisites: Soul Taste, Exclusive to Voracious Reading. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Your parasite's reading sharpens. You now roll 1d8 instead of 2d8 — but you choose which result you receive rather than rolling randomly.

## Voracious Reading
Prerequisites: Soul Taste, Exclusive to Refined Palate. 
Cost: 3 Trait Points. 
Your parasite drinks deeper from the target's signature. Roll 3d8 instead of 2d8. Duplicate rolls reveal nothing additional.
