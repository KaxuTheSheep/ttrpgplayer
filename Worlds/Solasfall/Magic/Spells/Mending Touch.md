
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana and direct into material
2. Identify break patterns at molecular level through mana resonance
3. Restore molecular bonds to previous configuration
4. Release mana

## Limitations
- Cannot repair magical items
- Cannot repair complex mechanisms (unless Enhancement invested)
- Material must be mostly intact
- Consumes no Stamina (unlike Lifebind)

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Area | 10cm³ | +10cm³ per point |
| Time | 1 round | -1 round per point (min 1 Tick) |
| Complexity | Simple | 2 points: complex mechanisms |
| Action Cost | 1 Action | -0.5 Actions per point |
| Priority | 4 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 6 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 10 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Object repaired but fragile: -2 Sturdiness until reinforced
- Object partially repaired: 1d4 damage remains
- Object disintegrates: releases shards dealing 1d4 damage to caster

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 6 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 10 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Repair is temporary: lasts 1 hour then reverts
- Object is discolored or deformed (cosmetic)
- Repair consumes double the normal area
