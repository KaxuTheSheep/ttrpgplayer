
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana
2. Create electrical potential difference between caster and target point
3. Force potential to equalize through directed current
4. On impact: overload target nervous system
5. Optional: maintain potential difference to sustain current (requires channeling)
6. Release mana

## Stun
Stun is applied on impact if target fails their Sturdiness Test.
- Sturdiness Test DN: 12 (modified by Enhancement)
- Metal armor: -2 to the Test
- Target loses Action(s) as determined by Enhancement

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Damage | 1d6 electrical | +1 die step per point |
| Range | 15m | +5m per point |
| Stun DN | 12 | +1 per point |
| Action Loss | 1 Action | 2 points: +1 Action lost |
| Leash Duration | None | 2 points: maintain tether (1d4 damage/round, no other Actions) |
| Priority | 7 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 9 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 13 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Arc grounds through caster's own equipment: 1 damage and stunned for 1 Tick
- Electrical feedback: caster takes half the spell's damage as feedback
- Uncontrolled discharge: arc strikes random target within range (including allies), caster loses 2 Actions while nervous system recovers

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 9 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 13 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Arc curves unpredictably: -2 to hit
- Stun duration reduced by half
- Damage type partially inverts (deals bludgeoning from kinetic force instead)
