
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana and direct through caster's soul
2. Create resonant pulse from soul into the environment
3. Amplify pulse outward
4. Receive and interpret returning pulse (mana resonance)
5. Release mana

## Limitations
- Detects mana concentrations, not physical objects
- Can be blocked by dense mana sources (interference)
- Does not detect creatures without mana (some constructs, undead)
- Cannot determine exact location, only direction and relative strength

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Range | 20m | +10m per point |
| Resolution | Vague (direction + strength) | 2 points: precise (can determine exact location) |
| Duration | 1 round | +1 round per point |
| Action Cost | 1 Action | -0.5 Actions per point |
| Priority | 5 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 8 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Pulse inverts: caster takes 1 Sanity damage from soul-resonance feedback
- Pulse reflects: caster loses sense of self for 1 round (-2 to all Actions)
- Pulse overload: caster gains 1 Exhaustion from excessive mana flow through soul

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 8 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Range reduced by half
- Resolution is vague regardless of Enhancement (can't pinpoint)
- Pulse returns false positives (mana sources that aren't there)
