
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana and shape into ground contact
2. Reverse sedimentary cohesion in target area
3. Force ground to flow upward around targets
4. Resolidify ground around targets
5. Release mana

## Limitations
- Requires solid ground in target area
- Soft earth becomes mud; hard stone becomes quicksand
- Immobilized targets can still act (cannot move)
- Takes 1 Action to break free (Strength Test DN: 12, modified by Enhancement)

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Area Radius | 3m | +1m per point |
| Break Free DN | 12 | +1 per point |
| Duration | 2 rounds | +1 round per point |
| Target Limit | All in area | 2 points: stone prison (encases, cannot act, Sturdiness 10) |
| Priority | 6 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 8 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Ground shifts under caster instead: caster must make Grace Test or fall prone
- Stone grasps caster's legs: immobilized for 1 round
- Ground erupts violently: 1d6 bludgeoning to everyone in area (including caster)

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 8 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Area smaller by half
- Duration reduced by half
- Targets can break free with half the Strength required
