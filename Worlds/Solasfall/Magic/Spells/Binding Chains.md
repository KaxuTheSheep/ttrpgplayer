
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana
2. Force kinetic energy to coalesce into physical form
3. Shape energy into chains
4. Direct chains toward target
5. On contact: wrap chains around target
6. Maintain chain solidity through continuous mana flow
7. Release mana

## Limitations
- Cannot be used to attack (restraint only)
- Chains are physically real but temporary
- Can target multiple creatures only if they are within 5m of each other

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Break Free DN | 12 | +1 per point |
| Duration | 2 rounds | +1 round per point |
| Range | 10m | +5m per point |
| Targets | 1 | +1 target per point (must be within 5m of each other) |
| Priority | 6 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 8 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Chains wrap around caster instead: caster is restrained for 1 round
- Chains are brittle: target breaks free automatically on their next turn
- Chains constrict violently: target takes 1d4 damage from pressure

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 8 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Duration reduced by half
- Break Free DN reduced by half
- Chains can only target one direction (cone, not omnidirectional)
