
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana and direct into living tissue
2. Identify cellular damage patterns through mana resonance
3. Accelerate natural cellular repair processes (mitosis, protein synthesis)
4. Supply energy for accelerated metabolism (consumes target's Stamina)
5. Release mana

## Limitations
- Cannot heal wounds older than 1 hour (cells are already fixed in scarred state)
- Consumes 1 Stamina from the target (or causes 1 Exhaustion if Stamina is 0)
- Requires skin-to-skin contact

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Healing | 1d4 HP | +1 die step per point |
| Targets | 1 | +1 target per point (must touch all) |
| Wound Age Limit | 1 hour | +1 hour per point |
| Action Cost | 1 Action | -0.5 Actions per point |
| Priority | 4 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 6 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 10 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Healing redirected to non-essential tissue: target heals but gains 1 Exhaustion from wasted energy
- Cellular overgrowth: target heals but gains minor mutation (GM determines, cosmetic or minor mechanical)
- Rapid necrosis: target takes 1d6 damage as cells are forced to divide and die simultaneously

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 6 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 10 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Healing reduced by half
- Target gains 1 Exhaustion from inefficient energy transfer
- Healing delayed by 1 round (takes effect at start of target's next turn)
