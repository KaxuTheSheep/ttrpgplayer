
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana and shape into a lance
2. Suppress thermal energy in a focused area to create cryogenic zone
3. Condense ambient moisture into solid form
4. Apply kinetic vector toward target
5. On impact: release thermal suppression, causing rapid heat absorption
6. Release mana

## Chill
Chill is applied on impact.
- Target loses 1 Action on their next turn
- Movement speed reduced by half
- Duration: 1 round (extended by additional Chill applications)
- Stacks with itself (each application extends duration by 1 round)
- Cannot be extended by Enhancement Points

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Damage | 1d8 cold | +1 die step per point |
| Range | 15m | +5m per point |
| Chill Duration | 1 round | +1 round per point |
| Penetration | Light cover | 2 points: ignores light cover |
| Action Cost | 2 Actions | -0.5 Actions per point |
| Priority | 6 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 7 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 11 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Frost crystallizes on caster's hands (-1 Grace until cleaned off, 1 round)
- Thermal inversion: target area is warmed (+1 Heat to affected creatures, no damage)
- Lance shatters in caster's grip: 1d4 cold damage and Chill applied to caster

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 7 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 11 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Projectile curves slightly on release
- Effective range reduced by fixed amount
- Chill duration reduced by half
- Damage type partially inverts (deals bludgeoning instead of cold from ice shards)
