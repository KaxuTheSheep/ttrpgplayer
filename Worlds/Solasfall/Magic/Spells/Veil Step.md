
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana and form spatial geometry
2. Stress local spatial membrane at origin point
3. Connect origin to destination through membrane stress
4. Move caster through the connection
5. Release the stress, allowing membrane to resolidify
6. Release mana

## Limitations
- Cannot pass through solid barriers more than 0.5m thick
- Leaves a visible distortion at both points (detectable)
- Caster cannot act again until their next round
- Requires line of sight to destination (unless Enhancement is invested)

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Range | 10m | +5m per point |
| Barrier Thickness | 0.5m | +0.5m per point |
| Line of Sight | Required | 2 points: can target any point within range (must accurately sense it) |
| Action Cost | 2 Actions | -0.5 Actions per point |
| Priority | 8 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 10 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 14 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Spatial displacement: emerge 1d3m off-target (GM determines direction)
- Spatial drag: caster's legs lag behind their torso in space — 1d4 physical damage and lose 1 Action next turn
- Spatial feedback: caster is trapped in the fold for 1 round, unable to act, emerging with 1d6 damage and 1 Exhaustion

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 10 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 14 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Afterimage visible (can be targeted; -2 to hit caster for 1 round)
- Destination off by 1d3m (GM determines direction)
- Caster appears at same location, taking 1d4 damage and gaining 1 Exhaustion
