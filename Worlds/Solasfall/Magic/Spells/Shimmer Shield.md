
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana
2. Suppress and amplify light wavelengths simultaneously
3. Force photons into dense wave pattern
4. Form wave pattern into a surface around caster
5. Maintain wave density through continuous mana flow
6. Release mana

## Limitations
- Does not block area effects — only targeted attacks
- Visually obvious (attracts attention)
- Can be maintained (Action cost each round)

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Defense Bonus | +4 | +2 per point |
| Duration | 2 rounds | +1 round per point |
| Area | Self | 2 points: affects one adjacent ally |
| Action Cost | 1 Action | -0.5 Actions per point |
| Priority | 5 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 8 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Shield flickers: becomes transparent at the wrong moments, granting no Defense for 1 round segment
- Light inversion: shield reflects inward, momentarily blinding the caster (-4 to all actions for 1 round)
- Prismatic shatter: shield explodes outward, dealing 1d4 light damage to caster and blinding creatures within 5m for 1 round

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 8 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 12 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Shield only blocks from one direction (GM determines which)
- Shield blocks outgoing attacks as well — caster cannot act outside the shield
- Shield collapses after 1 round, dealing 1d4 force damage to those inside
