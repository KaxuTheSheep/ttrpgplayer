
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana
2. Manipulate existing moisture in air and target
3. Increase hydrogen ion concentration to create acid
4. Apply kinetic vector toward target
5. On contact: release acid onto surface
6. Maintain acid through continuous mana flow
7. Release mana

## Corrosion
Corrosion is applied on impact.
- Deals 1d4 acid damage per round
- Damages armour: reduce armour by 1 tier every round. 
- Base duration: 2 rounds
- Can be removed through logical physical countermeasures (water, base neutralization)

| Variable | Base | Scaling |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Acid forms on caster's hands: 1d4 damage to caster
- Acid is basic instead of acidic: no damage (but stains equipment)
- Acid spray: damage to all creatures within 3m of caster
