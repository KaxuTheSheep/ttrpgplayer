
## Process
1. Draw ambient mana
2. Manipulate air density in a channel between caster and target
3. Amplify sound waves at one end of channel
4. Suppress sound wave diffusion
5. Maintain channel through continuous mana flow
6. Release mana

## Limitations
- Directional (choose a direction)
- Cannot be detected except through magical senses
- Sends or receives messages, not both simultaneously unless channel maintained

## Variables

| Variable | Base | Scaling |
|----------|------|---------|
| Range | 20m | +10m per point |
| Duration | 1 round | +1 round per point |
| Directionality | Directional | 2 points: omni-directional (broadcast only) |
| Action Cost | 1 Action | -0.5 Actions per point |
| Priority | 4 + Grace | +2 priority per point |

## Flux Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Control DN | 5 + Enhancement |
| Manifest DN | 9 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Backlash Examples:**
- Wind carries voice in the wrong direction: message goes to the wrong target
- Static interference: listeners hear only static
- Vacuum forms briefly: everyone in 5m loses breath for 1 round (-2 to Actions)

## Weave Casting

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Time | 5 + Enhancement (draw time) |
| Defect DN | 9 + (Enhancement × 2) |

**Defect Examples:**
- Message is garbled (requires Insight Test to interpret)
