# Armour Mastery
![[Armour Mastery.png]]
## Worn In
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Reduce the [[Solasfall Armour|Evasion]] Modifier penalty from any armour you wear by **1** (minimum 0 penalty reduction per piece — this cannot turn a penalty into a bonus).

## Broken In
**Prerequisites:** Worn In.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The [[Evasion]] Modifier reduction from Worn In increases to **2** total.

## Sure Stride
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Reduce the Walk and Run [[Movement Types|Movement]] penalty imposed by your worn armour by **1m** each.

## Steady Gait
**Prerequisites:** Sure Stride.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Reduce the Climb and Jump [[Movement Types|Movement]] penalty imposed by your worn armour by **1m** each, in addition to Sure Stride's Walk and Run reduction.

## Tempered Frame
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Reduce the effective [[Attributes|Might]] Requirement of any armour you wear by **1** for the purposes of determining whether you can use it effectively.

## Second Skin
**Prerequisites:** Tempered Frame.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The [[Attributes|Might]] Requirement reduction from Tempered Frame increases to **2** total.

## Armour Training
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You are considered **Trained** for the purposes of using [[Dodge]] in Half Plate or Full Plate — see [[Solasfall Armour]]. Without this node, Half Plate and Full Plate prevent Dodge entirely; with it, you gain the Trained benefits those armours list.

## Reinforced Movement
**Prerequisites:** Armour Training.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The **–1m distance moved** penalty that [[Dodge]] suffers while Trained in Chainmail, Half Plate, or Full Plate no longer applies to you.

## Full Mobility
**Prerequisites:** Reinforced Movement.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
While wearing Full Plate and Dodging, you use your full [[Attributes|Grace]] bonus to Evasion instead of the reduced Grace ÷ 3 (rounded down) that Full Plate normally imposes on a Trained wearer.

## Absorbing Plates
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
While wearing armour with a DR of 2 or higher, gain **+1 DR** against the first instance of physical [[Damage Types|damage]] you take each [[Rounds|Round]].

## Layered Protection
**Prerequisites:** Absorbing Plates.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Absorbing Plates' bonus increases to **+2 DR**, and now applies to the first **two** instances of physical [[Damage Types|damage]] you take each [[Rounds|Round]] rather than only the first.

## Deflecting Angles
**Prerequisites:** Broken In.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], when a melee [[Attacks|attack]] against you would be a Critical Success, you may make a [[Attributes|Grace]] Test (DN 14). On success, the [[Attacks|attack]] is treated as a normal success instead — your armour's angled surfaces turn what should have been a solid hit into a glancing one.

## Unshakeable Bearing
**Prerequisites:** Steady Gait, Second Skin.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
While wearing armour that meets its Might Requirement (after reductions from this tree), you have **Advantage** on Tests made to resist being knocked Prone or forcibly moved.

## Second Nature
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Full Mobility, Layered Protection, or Deflecting Angles.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Choose one armour tier (Brigandine, Chainmail, Half Plate, or Full Plate). While wearing armour of that specific tier, treat its [[Evasion]] Modifier and Movement penalties as one tier lighter (e.g. Full Plate behaves as Half Plate for these purposes only), stacking with all other reductions from this tree. This does not change the armour's DR, Might Requirement, or Dodge training requirement.
