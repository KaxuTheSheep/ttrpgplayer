# Grapple
![[Grapple.png]]
## Crushing Grip
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+2** to the opposed roll when initiating a Grapple.

## Vice Hold
**Prerequisites:** Crushing Grip.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+2** to the opposed roll whenever a creature you are Grappling attempts to escape.

## Takedown
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When you successfully initiate a Grapple, you may force the target to make a **Might Test** (DN 10 + your Might) as part of the same action. On failure, they also fall **Prone**.

## Ground Control
**Prerequisites:** Takedown.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
While a creature you are Grappling is Prone, your melee Attacks against them gain **Advantage**, in addition to any other bonus Prone already grants.

## Unshaken Grip
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You no longer suffer Disadvantage on Attacks made with **Reach** or **Cumbersome** weapons while maintaining a Grapple.

## Efficient Hold
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Maintaining a Grapple now costs a **Half Action** instead of a Full Action.

## Second Grip
**Prerequisites:** Efficient Hold.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Provided you have a free limb, you may initiate a second, independent Grapple against a different target. Maintaining both simultaneously still costs only a single Half Action (or Full Action, if you lack Efficient Hold).

## Suffocating Pressure
**Prerequisites:** Vice Hold.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
At the start of each of your turns while maintaining a Grapple, the target automatically takes **1d4 + Might** Blunt damage, bypassing Sturdiness.

## Improved Break
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+2** to your own Tests made to escape a Grapple.

## Slip the Hold
**Prerequisites:** Improved Break.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Once per Rest, you may attempt to escape a Grapple as a **Half Action** instead of a Full Action.

## Throw
**Prerequisites:** Ground Control.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
As a Full Action while maintaining a Grapple, you may throw the target up to **3m** in a direction of your choice into an unoccupied space, ending the Grapple. If they collide with a solid object or another creature, both parties take **1d6 + Might** Blunt damage and the thrown target falls **Prone**.

## Locked Bind
**Prerequisites:** Unshaken Grip.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
If a weapon you are wielding has the **Weapon Bind** trait, you may initiate that Bind and a standard Grapple against the same target with a single opposed roll. Both conditions end independently as normal, but escaping either requires beating the higher of your Might or Grace.

## Master Grappler
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Efficient Hold, Suffocating Pressure, or Throw.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
While you are maintaining a Grapple on a creature, you have **Advantage** on all Attack Tests made against them, and they cannot benefit from **Dodge** against your Attacks.
