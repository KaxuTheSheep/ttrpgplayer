# Pugilist
![[Pugilist.png]]
## Iron Fists
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Unarmed Attacks you make deal **+1 damage**.

## Callused Knuckles
**Prerequisites:** Iron Fists.
**Cost:** 1 Trait Point.
Your unarmed strikes are never treated as makeshift or improvised. You cannot be Disarmed while fighting unarmed, and unarmed Attacks ignore the first point of DR from any single source per hit (representing knuckle, elbow, and shin conditioning finding the gaps armour can't cover).

## Iron Body
**Prerequisites:** Iron Fists.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may declare **Block** while unarmed. Your Block Value becomes **1d4 + Vitality**, using your forearms and shins to intercept incoming force.

## Unbreakable
**Prerequisites:** Iron Body.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
While unarmed and wearing no armour heavier than Brigandine, reduce all incoming **Blunt** damage by **2**.

## Quickened Fists
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Unarmed Attacks you make gain **+1 to Speed**.

## Flurry
**Prerequisites:** Quickened Fists.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Once per Round, as part of the same Full Action as an unarmed Attack, you may make **one additional unarmed Attack** at **–2**.

## Relentless Combo
**Prerequisites:** Flurry.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
**Action Fatigue** no longer accumulates on unarmed special abilities or Forms used in consecutive Rounds.

## First Stance
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You gain access to the **Martial Arts** system. Learn **one** Martial Arts Style. Its Forms are available to you at all times as modifiers to your unarmed Attacks.

## Second Style
**Prerequisites:** First Stance, Level 3.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
You learn a second Martial Arts Style.

## Third Style
**Prerequisites:** Second Style, Level 5.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
You learn a third Martial Arts Style.

## Unbroken Fist
**Prerequisites:** Unbreakable, Relentless Combo.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
While your unarmed Block has not been reduced to 0 this Round, **Flurry**'s additional Attack no longer suffers the –2 penalty.

## Perfect Form
**Prerequisites:** Fifth Style, Relentless Combo.
**Cost:** 6 Trait Points.
Using a Form no longer triggers Action Fatigue at all, regardless of how many Styles you know or how often you switch between their Forms round to round.
