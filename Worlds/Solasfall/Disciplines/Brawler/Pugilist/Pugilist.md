# Pugilist
![[Pugilist.png]]
## Iron Fists
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Unarmed [[Attacks]] you make deal **+1 [[Damage Types|damage]]**.

## Callused Knuckles
**Prerequisites:** Iron Fists.
**Cost:** 1 Trait Point.
Your unarmed strikes are never treated as makeshift or improvised. You cannot be Disarmed while fighting unarmed, and unarmed [[Attacks]] ignore the first point of DR from any single source per hit (representing knuckle, elbow, and shin conditioning finding the gaps armour can't cover).

## Iron Body
**Prerequisites:** Iron Fists.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may declare **Block** while unarmed. Your Block Value becomes **1d4 + [[Attributes|Vitality]]**, using your forearms and shins to intercept incoming force.

## Unbreakable
**Prerequisites:** Iron Body.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
While unarmed and wearing no armour heavier than Brigandine, reduce all incoming **Blunt** [[Damage Types|damage]] by **2**.

## Quickened Fists
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Unarmed [[Attacks]] you make gain **+1 to [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]]**.

## Flurry
**Prerequisites:** Quickened Fists.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], as part of the same Full [[Actions|Action]] as an unarmed [[Attacks|Attack]], you may make **one additional unarmed [[Attacks|Attack]]** at **–2**.

## Relentless Combo
**Prerequisites:** Flurry.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
**[[Actions|Action]] Fatigue** no longer accumulates on unarmed special abilities or Forms used in consecutive [[Rounds]].

## First Stance
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You gain access to the **Martial Arts** system. Learn **one** Martial Arts Style. Its Forms are available to you at all times as modifiers to your unarmed [[Attacks]].

## Second Style
**Prerequisites:** First Stance, Level 3.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
You learn a second Martial Arts Style.

## Third Style
**Prerequisites:** Second Style, Level 5.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
You learn a third Martial Arts Style.

## Unbroken Fist
**Prerequisites:** Unbreakable, Relentless Combo.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
While your unarmed Block has not been reduced to 0 this [[Rounds|Round]], **Flurry**'s additional [[Attacks|Attack]] no longer suffers the –2 penalty.

## Perfect Form
**Prerequisites:** Fifth Style, Relentless Combo.
**Cost:** 6 Trait Points.
Using a Form no longer triggers [[Actions|Action]] Fatigue at all, regardless of how many Styles you know or how often you switch between their Forms [[Rounds|round]] to [[Rounds|round]].
