# Crossbows
![[Crossbows.png]]

## Practised Hands
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 1 Trait Point.
Reloading a crossbow with the **Crossbow Loading** trait no longer costs a Half [[Actions|Action]] — it happens automatically as part of firing. This applies once per [[Rounds|Round]].

## Steady Grip
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 1 Trait Point.
You no longer suffer **Disadvantage** from firing a crossbow within its Minimum [[Range & Threat|Range]].

## Held Bolt
**Prerequisites:** Practised Hands.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may declare a **Ready [[Actions|Action]]** with a loaded crossbow without needing to reload again once it triggers — the bolt stays seated and ready through [[Movement Types|movement]] or a held stance.

## True Aim
**Prerequisites:** Steady Grip.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+1** to [[Attacks|Attack]] Tests made with a crossbow.

## Punishing Bolt
**Prerequisites:** True Aim.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Increase any Collateral value your crossbow has by **1**. If it has none, it gains **Collateral(1)**.

## Marksman's Trigger
**Prerequisites:** Held Bolt, True Aim.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When you score a **Critical Success** with a crossbow, **Practised Hands**' free reload applies even if you've already used it this [[Rounds|Round]], allowing you to fire again immediately at no [[Actions|Action]] cost.

## Unshakeable Line
**Prerequisites:** Marksman's Trigger.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Reduce the [[Attacks|Attack]] penalty from firing beyond a crossbow's optimal [[Range & Threat|range]] by **1 per 20m increment** instead of the standard rate (i.e. the penalty is effectively halved).
