# Whip
![[Whips.png]]
## Snap Guard
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You no longer suffer Disadvantage on **Parry** Tests while a creature is within your Close-Quarters range. The Attack Disadvantage from [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]]'s Close-In Penalty still applies.

## Practiced Coil
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+2** to any opposed roll made during a [[Weapon Traits#Weapon Bind|Weapon Bind]] Disarm Attempt with this weapon.

## Coil the Ground
**Prerequisites:** Snap Guard.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Creatures that begin their turn within your melee threat range suffer **–1** to their Initiation Speed for movement Actions taken that Round.

## Second Coil
**Prerequisites:** Snap Guard.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may perform a **Reach Punish** a second time in the same Round, provided the second Punish targets a different creature than the first.

## Tightening Grip
**Prerequisites:** Practiced Coil.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
While maintaining a Bind with this weapon, your Disarm Attempt no longer costs a Full Action — it costs a **Half Action** instead.

## Superior Weapon Bind
**Prerequisites:** Practiced Coil.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
This weapon's **Weapon Bind** trait no longer requires a successful Parry to initiate. Instead, when you hit a target with a melee Attack using this weapon, you may forgo dealing damage and force a **Bind Contest**: opposed Might or Grace (your choice) vs the target's Might or Grace. On success, initiate a Bind as normal. On failure, no damage is dealt and no Bind occurs — the whip simply fails to catch.

## Wrenching Pull
**Prerequisites:** Tightening Grip.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
As a **Half Action** while maintaining a Bind, force a Might Test (DN 10 + your Might) on the bound creature or weapon. On failure, drag the bound target or their weapon up to **2m** toward you, provided the space is unoccupied and unobstructed.

## Choke Point
**Prerequisites:** Tightening Grip.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
As a **Full Action** while maintaining a Bind, deal **1d6 + Grace** damage directly to the bound creature, bypassing Sturdiness. This represents the coil cinching tight rather than a conventional strike.

## Held Ground
**Prerequisites:** Coil the Ground, Second Coil.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your **Reach Punish** also inflicts Coil the Ground's Speed penalty on the target directly, regardless of whether they remain in your threat range afterward.

## Coil the Body
**Prerequisites:** Superior Weapon Bind.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your Bind Contest may target a creature directly instead of their weapon. On success, the target gains the **Grappled** condition (per standard [[Grapple]] rules) instead of Bound. **You**, however, gain the **Bound** condition yourself for the duration — the whip is wrapped around them, and holding the coil taut occupies your weapon entirely, denying you its use for Attacks, Parry, or Block. You may release the coil at any time as a free action, ending your own Bound state — the target remains Grappled until they escape normally or you otherwise release them.

## Snapped Coil
**Prerequisites:** Held Ground, Superior Weapon Bind.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
When a creature enters your melee threat range, you may forgo your normal **Reach Punish** attack roll and instead immediately force a Bind Contest against them, targeting either their weapon or (with Coil the Body) the creature itself. This still costs your Reaction as normal for a Reach Punish.

## Unshakeable Coil
**Prerequisites:** Snapped Coil, Coil the Body.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
While maintaining a Bind established through this tree, you cannot be Disarmed, and the bound target suffers Disadvantage on all Tests made to escape or break free.

## Parting Strike
**Prerequisites:** Snapped Coil, Coil the Body.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Once per Round, when you voluntarily end a Bind established through this tree, you may immediately make a free melee Attack against the creature you released, at no Action cost.
