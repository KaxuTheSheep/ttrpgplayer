# Whips
![[Whips.png]]
## Snap Guard
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You no longer suffer Disadvantage on **Parry** Tests while a creature is within your Close-Quarters [[Range & Threat|range]]. The [[Attacks|Attack]] Disadvantage from [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]]'s Close-In Penalty still applies.

## Practiced Coil
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+2** to any opposed roll made during a [[Weapon Traits#Weapon Bind|Weapon Bind]] Disarm Attempt with this [[Weapon|weapon]].

## Coil the Ground
**Prerequisites:** Snap Guard.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Creatures that begin their turn within your melee threat [[Range & Threat|range]] suffer **–1** to their Initiation [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] for [[Movement Types|movement]] [[Actions]] taken that [[Rounds|Round]].

## Second Coil
**Prerequisites:** Snap Guard.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may perform a **Reach Punish** a second time in the same [[Rounds|Round]], provided the second Punish targets a different creature than the first.

## Tightening Grip
**Prerequisites:** Practiced Coil.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
While maintaining a Bind with this [[Weapon|weapon]], your Disarm Attempt no longer costs a Full [[Actions|Action]] — it costs a **Half [[Actions|Action]]** instead.

## Superior [[Weapon]] Bind
**Prerequisites:** Practiced Coil.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
This [[Weapon|weapon]]'s **[[Weapon]] Bind** trait no longer requires a successful Parry to initiate. Instead, when you hit a target with a melee [[Attacks|Attack]] using this [[Weapon|weapon]], you may forgo dealing [[Damage Types|damage]] and force a **Bind Contest**: opposed [[Attributes|Might]] or [[Attributes|Grace]] (your choice) vs the target's [[Attributes|Might]] or [[Attributes|Grace]]. On success, initiate a Bind as normal. On failure, no [[Damage Types|damage]] is dealt and no Bind occurs — the whip simply fails to catch.

## Wrenching Pull
**Prerequisites:** Tightening Grip.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
As a **Half [[Actions|Action]]** while maintaining a Bind, force a [[Attributes|Might]] Test (DN 10 + your [[Attributes|Might]]) on the bound creature or [[Weapon|weapon]]. On failure, drag the bound target or their [[Weapon|weapon]] up to **2m** toward you, provided the space is unoccupied and unobstructed.

## Choke Point
**Prerequisites:** Tightening Grip.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
As a **Full [[Actions|Action]]** while maintaining a Bind, deal **1d6 + [[Attributes|Grace]]** [[Damage Types|damage]] directly to the bound creature, bypassing Sturdiness. This represents the coil cinching tight rather than a conventional strike.

## Held Ground
**Prerequisites:** Coil the Ground, Second Coil.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your **Reach Punish** also inflicts Coil the Ground's [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] penalty on the target directly, regardless of whether they remain in your threat [[Range & Threat|range]] afterward.

## Coil the Body
**Prerequisites:** Superior [[Weapon]] Bind.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your Bind Contest may target a creature directly instead of their [[Weapon|weapon]]. On success, the target gains the **Grappled** condition (per standard [[Core/Combat/Action System/Grapple]] rules) instead of Bound. **You**, however, gain the **Bound** condition yourself for the duration — the whip is wrapped around them, and holding the coil taut occupies your [[Weapon|weapon]] entirely, denying you its use for [[Attacks]], Parry, or Block. You may release the coil at any time as a free [[Actions|action]], ending your own Bound state — the target remains Grappled until they escape normally or you otherwise release them.

## Snapped Coil
**Prerequisites:** Held Ground, Superior [[Weapon]] Bind.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
When a creature enters your melee threat [[Range & Threat|range]], you may forgo your normal **Reach Punish** [[Attacks|attack]] roll and instead immediately force a Bind Contest against them, targeting either their [[Weapon|weapon]] or (with Coil the Body) the creature itself. This still costs your Reaction as normal for a Reach Punish.

## Unshakeable Coil
**Prerequisites:** Snapped Coil, Coil the Body.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
While maintaining a Bind established through this tree, you cannot be Disarmed, and the bound target suffers Disadvantage on all Tests made to escape or break free.

## Parting Strike
**Prerequisites:** Snapped Coil, Coil the Body.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], when you voluntarily end a Bind established through this tree, you may immediately make a free melee [[Attacks|Attack]] against the creature you released, at no [[Actions|Action]] cost.
