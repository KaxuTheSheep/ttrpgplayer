# Bows
![[Bows.png]]
## Held Draw
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You may declare a **Ready Action** with a bow set to fire a **Heavy Attack**, holding the full draw without re-drawing once it triggers — mirroring how a crossbow's Held Bolt keeps a bolt seated, but for a longbow's held pull.

## Bodkin Point
**Prerequisites:** Held Draw.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You may switch the damage type of your bow's ammunition to **Piercing** at will, at no cost, and ignore **1** point of the target's Armour DR on a hit.

## Punishing Draw
**Prerequisites:** Bodkin Point.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When you deal damage to a target wearing Armour, you may trigger a Sundering effect as though your bow had the **Sundering** trait, even if it does not.

## Warbow Conditioning
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Reduce the effective Might Requirement of any bow you wield by **1**, and halve any Disadvantage or penalty imposed by falling short of a bow's Might Requirement.

## Killing Draw
**Prerequisites:** Punishing Draw, Warbow Conditioning.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Once per Rest, a **Heavy Attack** made with a bow rolls on the Critical Success Table on a roll of natural 9 or 10 (instead of only double 10s), and deals an additional weapon damage die if it hits.

## Weak Point
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
As part of an Attack declaration, you may call a shot against a specific location associated with a Permanent Injury (see Injuries). Your Attack Test suffers a **–4** penalty. On a hit, in addition to normal damage, the GM may apply narrative or mechanical effects consistent with that location.

## String Walking
**Prerequisites:** Weak Point.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Reduce the range penalty rate for bows you wield by **1 per 20m increment** instead of the standard rate — effectively halving range penalties.

## Anchor Point
**Prerequisites:** String Walking.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You no longer suffer Disadvantage from firing a bow within its Minimum Range.

## Practised Eye
**Prerequisites:** Weak Point.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **Advantage** on Perception Tests made to judge distance, wind, or trajectory before making a bow Attack this Round.

## Dead Aim
**Prerequisites:** Anchor Point, Practised Eye.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
You gain **Advantage** on Attack Tests made using Weak Point's called shots.

## Snap Loose
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Once per Round, nocking and loosing an arrow no longer costs any part of your Attack Action beyond the Attack itself — you may draw an additional arrow from your quiver as a free action immediately after firing.

## Quickened Draw
**Prerequisites:** Snap Loose.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Bow Attacks you make gain **+1 to Priority**.

## Volley Arc
**Prerequisites:** Quickened Draw.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your bow Attack may instead target a cone extending out to the bow's optimal Range in front of you, covering your Front Arc and one Side Arc (90° total). Make a single Attack roll, compared individually against the Evasion of every creature in the cone. Damage die is reduced by one step for each target hit beyond the first, to a minimum of 0 damage.

## Storm of Arrows
**Prerequisites:** Volley Arc.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
The damage penalty from Volley Arc softens. Your damage die is now reduced by one step for every **two** targets hit beyond the first, rounded down, instead of every one.

## Arcing Fire
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Once per Round, a bow Attack you make may ignore Partial or Significant Cover entirely, representing a lobbed, high-arcing shot dropped over an obstruction.

## Quiver Discipline
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You recover **+50%** additional ammunition when retrieving arrows after combat (round up), and require half the usual time to restock a quiver during a Rest.

## Braced Stance
**Prerequisites:** Quiver Discipline.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You no longer suffer Disadvantage on bow Attack Tests made while on unstable footing, at height, or without a stable stance, provided you have not moved this Round.

## Wind Reading
**Prerequisites:** Practised Eye.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Negate any range or Attack penalty imposed by adverse environmental conditions (wind, rain, poor light affecting trajectory) on your bow Attacks, at GM discretion for applicability.

## Stalker's Patience
**Prerequisites:** Braced Stance.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
If you have held Aim (via Ready Action or otherwise remained stationary targeting the same creature) for a full Round without firing, your next bow Attack against that target gains **+3 damage**.

## Flight Shot
**Prerequisites:** Killing Draw.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Your bow's optimal Range increases by **50%**, and you ignore the first 20m increment of range penalty entirely.

## Master Archer
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Dead Aim, Storm of Arrows, or Killing Draw.
**Cost:** 5 Trait Points.
You gain **Advantage** on bow Attack Tests against any creature that has not yet acted this Round.

## Perfect Loose
**Prerequisites:** Master Archer, Flight Shot.
**Cost:** 6 Trait Points.
Once per Rest, make a bow Attack that **ignores Armour DR entirely** and **cannot be Parried or Blocked**. Dodge still applies normally.
