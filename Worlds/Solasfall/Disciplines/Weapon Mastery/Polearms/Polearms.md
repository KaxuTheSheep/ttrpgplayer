# Polearms 
![[Polearms.png]]
## Measured Distance 
**Prerequisites:** None. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
You no longer suffer Disadvantage on **Parry** Tests while a creature is within your Close-Quarters [[Range & Threat|range]]. The [[Attacks|Attack]] Disadvantage from [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]]'s Close-In Penalty still applies. 
## Anchor Point 
**Prerequisites:** None. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Polearms you wield can be drawn or stowed as a **Half [[Actions|Action]]** instead of a Full [[Actions|Action]], even if the [[Weapon|weapon]] has the [[Weapon Traits#Cumbersome|Cumbersome]] trait. 
## Ground to Hold 
**Prerequisites:** Measured Distance. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Creatures that begin their turn within your melee threat [[Range & Threat|range]] suffer **–1** to their Initiation [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] for [[Movement Types|Movement]] taken that [[Rounds|Round]]. 
## Guard the Line 
**Prerequisites:** Measured Distance.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may perform a **Reach Punish** a second time in the same [[Rounds|Round]], provided the second Punish targets a different creature than the first.
## Braced Stance 
**Prerequisites:** Anchor Point. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
While you have not moved this [[Rounds|Round]], melee [[Attacks]] you make with this [[Weapon|weapon]] deal **+1 [[Damage Types|damage]]**. 
## Closing the Distance 
**Prerequisites:** Anchor Point.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The first time each [[Rounds|Round]] a creature enters your Close-Quarters [[Range & Threat|range]], your next melee [[Attacks|Attack]] against them this [[Rounds|Round]] ignores Reach's Close-In [[Attacks|Attack]] Disadvantage. 
## Held Ground 
**Prerequisites:** Ground to Hold, Guard the Line.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your **Reach Punish** also inflicts Ground to Hold's [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] penalty on the target direction regardless of whether they remain in your threat [[Range & Threat|range]] afterward. 
## Timed Strike
**Prerequisites:** Guard the Line, Braced Stance.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. If you have not moved this [[Rounds|Round]], your **Reach Punish** deals **one additional [[Weapon|weapon]] [[Damage Types|damage]] die**. 
## Adaptive Grip
**Prerequisites:** Braced Stance, Closing the Distance. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. You may gain the benefit of Braced Stance and Closing the Distance simultaneously for the [[Rounds|Round]], provided their individual conditions (not having moved; a creature entering your Close-Quarters [[Range & Threat|range]]) are both met.
## Denied Ground 
**Prerequisites:** Ground to Hold. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
While a creature remains within your melee threat [[Range & Threat|range]], halve their [[Movement Types|movement]] Travel Rate for any [[Movement Types|movement]] that keeps them within that threat [[Range & Threat|range]]. 
## Turned Aside
**Prerequisites:** Guard the Line. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When your Reach Punish hits, you may push the target **1m** directly away from you, provided the space is unoccupied and unobstructed. No Test required. 
## Immovable 
**Prerequisites:** Braced Stance.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. 
While you have not moved this [[Rounds|Round]], you have **Advantage** on Tests to resist being knocked Prone, Grappled, or forcibly moved. 
## Collapsed Guard 
**Prerequisites:** Closing the Distance. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. 
As a declared stance (**Half [[Actions|Action]]**), you may collapse your grip on this [[Weapon|weapon]]. Until you change stance: - The [[Weapon|weapon]] is treated as if it lacked the [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]] trait entirely for the purposes of the Close-In Penalty. - While a creature remains within your Close-Quarters [[Range & Threat|range]], your melee [[Attacks]] with this [[Weapon|weapon]] gain **Advantage** instead of suffering Disadvantage. - You lose access to **Reach Punish** while collapsed. 
## Unbroken Line
**Prerequisites:** Denied Ground. **Exclusive to Swift Reversal.**
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Allies within your melee threat [[Range & Threat|range]] benefit from Denied Ground as if they threatened that space themselves — a creature cannot move at full [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] to disengage from an ally standing beside you directly, 
## Swift Reversal 
**Prerequisites:** Collapsed Guard. **Exclusive to Unbroken Line.**
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Entering or leaving Collapsed Guard's stance no longer costs an [[Actions|action]], up to once per [[Rounds|Round]]. 
## Master of the Line
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Held Ground, Timed Strike, or Adaptive Grip.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points. 
The first time each [[Rounds|Round]] you perform a **Reach Punish**, it no longer costs your Reaction. Additionally, this [[Weapon|weapon]]'s [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]] value increases by **+0.5m**.`
