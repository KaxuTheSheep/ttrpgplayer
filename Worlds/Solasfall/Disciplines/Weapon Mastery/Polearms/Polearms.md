# Polearms 
![[Polearms.png]]
## Measured Distance 
**Prerequisites:** None. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
You no longer suffer Disadvantage on **Parry** Tests while a creature is within your Close-Quarters range. The Attack Disadvantage from [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]]'s Close-In Penalty still applies. 
## Anchor Point 
**Prerequisites:** None. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Polearms you wield can be drawn or stowed as a **Half Action** instead of a Full Action, even if the weapon has the [[Weapon Traits#Cumbersome|Cumbersome]] trait. 
## Ground to Hold 
**Prerequisites:** Measured Distance. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Creatures that begin their turn within your melee threat range suffer **–1** to their Initiation Speed for movement Actions taken that Round. 
## Guard the Line 
**Prerequisites:** Measured Distance.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may perform a **Reach Punish** a second time in the same Round, provided the second Punish targets a different creature than the first.
## Braced Stance 
**Prerequisites:** Anchor Point. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
While you have not moved this Round, melee Attacks you make with this weapon deal **+1 damage**. 
## Closing the Distance 
**Prerequisites:** Anchor Point.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The first time each Round a creature enters your Close-Quarters range, your next melee Attack against them this Round ignores Reach's Close-In Attack Disadvantage. 
## Held Ground 
**Prerequisites:** Ground to Hold, Guard the Line.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your **Reach Punish** also inflicts Ground to Hold's Speed penalty on the target directly, regardless of whether they remain in your threat range afterward. 
## Timed Strike
**Prerequisites:** Guard the Line, Braced Stance.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. If you have not moved this Round, your **Reach Punish** deals **one additional weapon damage die**. 
## Adaptive Grip
**Prerequisites:** Braced Stance, Closing the Distance. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. You may gain the benefit of Braced Stance and Closing the Distance simultaneously for the Round, provided their individual conditions (not having moved; a creature entering your Close-Quarters range) are both met.
## Denied Ground 
**Prerequisites:** Ground to Hold. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
While a creature remains within your melee threat range, halve their movement Travel Rate for any movement that keeps them within that threat range. 
## Turned Aside
**Prerequisites:** Guard the Line. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When your Reach Punish hits, you may push the target **1m** directly away from you, provided the space is unoccupied and unobstructed. No Test required. 
## Immovable 
**Prerequisites:** Braced Stance.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. 
While you have not moved this Round, you have **Advantage** on Tests to resist being knocked Prone, Grappled, or forcibly moved. 
## Collapsed Guard 
**Prerequisites:** Closing the Distance. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. 
As a declared stance (**Half Action**), you may collapse your grip on this weapon. Until you change stance: - The weapon is treated as if it lacked the [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]] trait entirely for the purposes of the Close-In Penalty. - While a creature remains within your Close-Quarters range, your melee Attacks with this weapon gain **Advantage** instead of suffering Disadvantage. - You lose access to **Reach Punish** while collapsed. 
## Unbroken Line
**Prerequisites:** Denied Ground. **Exclusive to Swift Reversal.**
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Allies within your melee threat range benefit from Denied Ground as if they threatened that space themselves — a creature cannot move at full speed to disengage from an ally standing beside you either. 
## Swift Reversal 
**Prerequisites:** Collapsed Guard. **Exclusive to Unbroken Line.**
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Entering or leaving Collapsed Guard's stance no longer costs an action, up to once per Round. 
## Master of the Line
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Held Ground, Timed Strike, or Adaptive Grip.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points. 
The first time each Round you perform a **Reach Punish**, it no longer costs your Reaction. Additionally, this weapon's [[Weapon Traits#Reach|Reach]] value increases by **+0.5m**.`
