# Maces & Hammers
![[Maces & Hammers.png]]

## Skull-Rattler
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When you hit a target with a mace or hammer for damage equal to or greater than half their maximum Sturdiness, they must make a **Vitality Test (DN 12)** or gain **Dazed**.

## Concussive Follow-Up
**Prerequisites:** Skull-Rattler.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The DN of Skull-Rattler's Test increases to **14**. On failure, the target also suffers **Disadvantage** on their next Parry Test.

## Bell-Ringer
**Prerequisites:** Concussive Follow-Up.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When you score a **Critical Success** with a mace or hammer, you may impose **Stunned** for 1 Round instead of rolling on the standard Critical Success Table.

## Earthshaker
**Prerequisites:** Bell-Ringer.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When a **Heavy Attack** with a mace or hammer hits, every creature within 2m of the target (other than yourself) must make a **Vitality Test (DN 12)** or gain **Dazed**.

## Guard Crumple
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When you hit a target who is currently **Blocking** with a mace or hammer, roll your weapon's **Crushing** effect **twice** and apply the higher result.

## Collapsing Guard
**Prerequisites:** Guard Crumple.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
If your **Crushing** effect reduces a target's Block Value to 0, they also gain **Vulnerable** until the end of their next turn.

## Guard Breaker
**Prerequisites:** Collapsing Guard.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When you hit a target who is currently **Blocking**, you may forgo dealing damage to immediately end their Block outright, regardless of remaining Block Value.

## Merciless Opening
**Prerequisites:** Guard Breaker.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Immediately after you end a Block via **Guard Breaker**, you may make one free melee Attack against that target at no Action cost, resolving at the Speed the Block ended.

## Full Wind-Up
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
**Heavy Attacks** made with a mace or hammer suffer only **–1** to Speed instead of –2.

## Follow Through
**Prerequisites:** Full Wind-Up.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Heavy Attacks made with a mace or hammer cost **1 Full Action** instead of 2. They still deal double damage as normal.

## Bone Breaker
**Prerequisites:** Follow Through.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Once per Round, you may forgo your ability to Parry or Dodge until the start of your next turn to add your **Might a second time** to a single mace or hammer Attack's damage.

## Juggernaut
**Prerequisites:** Bone Breaker.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Heavy Attacks made with a mace or hammer no longer reduce Speed at all.

## Pulverising Blow
**Prerequisites:** Guard Breaker, Bell-Ringer.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
When you end a Block via **Guard Breaker**, the target immediately suffers **Skull-Rattler**'s effect with no Test allowed — they are simply Dazed.

## Cataclysmic Swing
**Prerequisites:** Earthshaker, Juggernaut.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
When a Heavy Attack triggers **Earthshaker**, creatures caught in its radius who fail their Vitality Test gain **Stunned** for 1 Round instead of Dazed.
