# Defender
![[Defender.png]]
## Braced Guard
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+1** to your [[Block]] Value.

## Reinforced Guard
**Prerequisites:** Braced Guard.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The bonus from Braced Guard increases to **+2** total.

## Wide Stance
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Your [[Block]] no longer only applies to your Front Arc — it also applies to [[Facing|attacks]] from your Left and Right Arcs.

## Full Coverage
**Prerequisites:** Wide Stance.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your [[Block]] now applies from **any** [[Facing|arc]], including your Rear Arc.

## Fluid Guard
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may move up to your **full** Walk Travel Rate while maintaining [[Block]], instead of half.

## Shield Bash
**Prerequisites:** None. Requires a wielded shield.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
As a **Half [[Actions|Action]]**, make an [[Attacks|attack]] with your shield: roll **2d10 + [[Attributes|Might]]** against the target's [[Evasion]]. On a hit, deal your shield's Block Die as Blunt [[Damage Types|damage]] and force a [[Attributes|Might]] Test (DN 10 + your [[Attributes|Might]]) or the target is knocked **Prone**.

## Punishing Block
**Prerequisites:** Shield Bash.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Whenever your [[Block]] reduces an incoming hit's [[Damage Types|damage]] to 0 before [[Sturdiness]], you may immediately make a free **Shield Bash** at no [[Actions|Action]] cost, resolving at the [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] the [[Block]] triggered.

## Retaliatory Guard
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Whenever your [[Block]] reduces an incoming hit's [[Damage Types|damage]] to 0 before [[Sturdiness]], you may make a free counter-[[Attacks|attack]] with your [[Weapon|weapon]] at **–2**, resolving at the [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] the [[Block]] triggered. This does not consume your [[Actions|Action]] economy, but you cannot combine it with Punishing Block's free Shield Bash on the same trigger — choose one.

## Steady Recovery
**Prerequisites:** Braced Guard.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Once per Rest, if a single hit would reduce your [[Block]] Value to 0 for the remainder of the [[Rounds|Round]] (such as from the **Crushing** [[Weapon Traits|trait]]), you may immediately restore it to its full value as a free [[Actions|action]].

## Overlapping Defence
**Prerequisites:** Wide Stance.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
While an ally is within your melee threat [[Range & Threat|range]] and you are both maintaining [[Block]], you may grant them **+2** to their [[Block]] Value instead of applying it to your own for that [[Rounds|Round]].

## Weight Behind It
**Prerequisites:** Shield Bash, 3 [[Attributes|Might]].
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your Shield Bash [[Attacks|attack]] deals **+2** [[Damage Types|damage]] and pushes the target **1m** directly away from you on a hit, in addition to its normal Prone Test.

## Unrelenting Guard
**Prerequisites:** Reinforced Guard, 4 [[Attributes|Might]].
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
The bonus from Braced Guard and Reinforced Guard increases to **+4** total, reflecting the sheer physical strength needed to hold a guard against the heaviest blows.

## Bulwark Stance
**Prerequisites:** Full Coverage, 5 [[Attributes|Might]].
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
As a **Full [[Actions|Action]]**, you may root yourself in place: until the start of your next turn, your [[Block]] Value is doubled, but you cannot voluntarily move for the duration (forced [[Movement Types|movement]] still applies normally).

## Unbroken Wall
**Prerequisites:** Reinforced Guard, Full Coverage.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your [[Block]] no longer needs to be redeclared each [[Rounds|Round]] — once declared, it remains active on subsequent Rounds until you voluntarily switch [[Defensive Stances|stance]], are incapacitated, or choose to stop.

## Immovable Object
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Punishing Block, Retaliatory Guard, Overlapping Defence, or Unbroken Wall.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Your [[Block]] Value can no longer be reduced by the **Crushing** [[Weapon Traits|trait]] or similar effects that specifically target Block DR. Additionally, while [[Block]] is active you have **Advantage** on Tests to resist being knocked Prone or forcibly moved.
