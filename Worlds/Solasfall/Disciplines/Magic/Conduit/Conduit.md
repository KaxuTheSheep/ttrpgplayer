# Conduit
![[Conduit.png]]
## Steady Current
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+1** to Flux Casting rolls (2d10 + [[Attributes|Insight]]).

## Sustained Current
**Prerequisites:** Steady Current.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The bonus from Steady Current increases to **+2** total.

## Environmental Attunement
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When casting a spell whose effect thematically matches an environmental source within your reach or line of sight (an open flame for a fire spell, a body of water for a water spell, and so on), you may draw the spell's initial shaping from that source instead of raw ambient [[Mana|mana]]. Reduce the spell's Control DN by **2** for that cast — you are accelerating an existing process rather than starting one from nothing.

## Deep Attunement
**Prerequisites:** Environmental Attunement.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Environmental Attunement's Control DN reduction increases to **4**, and also reduces the spell's Manifest DN by **2** for that cast.

## Efficient Casting
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The first Enhancement Point you invest in a Flux spell each cast does not increase its Control DN, though it still increases Manifest DN as normal.

## Overcharged Casting
**Prerequisites:** Efficient Casting.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Efficient Casting's exemption extends to your **second** Enhancement Point as well.

## Backlash Control
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When a cast of yours falls below Control DN, you may choose the specific form your Backlash takes from among the thematically appropriate options the GM would otherwise roll or pick from — you no longer lose control of your failures, only of your success.

## Contained Backlash
**Prerequisites:** Backlash Control.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
The [[Damage Types|damage]], radius, or severity of any Backlash you cause is halved, rounded down.

## Mana Sense
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
You may spend a Half [[Actions|Action]] to passively read the ambient [[Mana|mana]] currents in your immediate surroundings, sensing concentrations, unusual excitation, or recent significant casting activity within roughly 10m, at GM discretion for detail.

## Sensed Weakness
**Prerequisites:** Mana Sense.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Using Mana Sense against a specific creature you can see, you may attempt an opposed [[Attributes|Insight]] Test against their [[Attributes|Resolve]]. On success, you learn whether they are currently a conductor (mage, Follower, or similar) and roughly how much [[Mana|mana]] they have recently channelled.

## Forceful Shaping
**Prerequisites:** Sustained Current.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], you may accept a **+2** penalty to a Flux spell's Control DN in exchange for **+3** to your casting roll for that cast — deliberately overpowering the shaping rather than refining it.

## Unshaken Conductor
**Prerequisites:** Overcharged Casting, Contained Backlash.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
When you fail a Flux cast, you no longer suffer any secondary consequence beyond the Backlash itself (no [[Sanity]] loss, Exhaustion, or similar side effects some abilities or GM rulings might otherwise attach to failed casting) — the mishap is contained entirely to the Backlash's own effect.
