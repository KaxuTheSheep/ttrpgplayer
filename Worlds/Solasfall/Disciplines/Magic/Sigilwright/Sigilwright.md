# Sigilwright

## Precise Lines
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+1** to your Defect Test (2d10 + [[Attributes|Insight]] + [[Attributes|Grace]]).

## Exacting Lines
**Prerequisites:** Precise Lines.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The bonus from Precise Lines increases to **+2** total.

## Swift Draw
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Reduce the draw time of any Weave circle you construct by **1 Priority step**, to a minimum of 1.

## Practiced Hand
**Prerequisites:** Swift Draw.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Swift Draw's reduction increases to **2 Priority steps** total.

## Composure Under Draw
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], when you are successfully attacked while mid-draw on a Drawn Weave, you do not add the usual **+1 [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]] step** to your remaining draw time from that interruption. You must still make the associated Defect Test as normal.

## Battle Inscription
**Prerequisites:** Composure Under Draw.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When an interruption forces you to re-roll your Defect Test, you may roll **twice** and take the better result, rather than the worst result standing as normal.

## Conditional Weaving
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You may encode a single conditional trigger into a Woven circle at the time of drawing — a stated condition under which the circle activates ("if a creature crosses this threshold," "if struck by fire") rather than firing immediately or on a simple mechanical trigger. This does not count as an Enhancement Point investment and does not affect Control DN or Manifest DN on its own, though the GM may set a higher Manifest DN if the condition itself is complex or unreliable to detect.

## Layered Conditions
**Prerequisites:** Conditional Weaving.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
A Woven circle you draw may carry a **second** independent conditional trigger, either as an alternative activation path or a required companion condition to the first, your choice at the time of drawing.

## Efficient Inscription
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The first Enhancement Point you invest in a Weave circle does not increase its Manifest DN, though it still increases Control DN as normal.

## Reinforced Circuit
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Woven circles and [[Magic]] tools you personally construct are treated as one [[Equipment Quality & Degradation|Quality Tier]] higher for the sole purpose of resisting attempts to physically destroy or magically disrupt their circle structure.

## Unbroken Geometry
**Prerequisites:** Exacting Lines, Efficient Inscription.
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Once per Long [[Rest & Recovery|Rest]], a Woven circle you complete may treat its Defect Test result as though it met the Manifest DN exactly, regardless of the actual roll — guaranteeing at worst a cosmetic, flavour-only Defect on that one working.
