# Purifier

## Mana Disruption
**Prerequisites:** None.  
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When you witness a creature within line of sight begin casting a Flux spell, you may spend your Reaction to attempt disruption. Make an opposed Insight Test against the caster's Insight + the spell's Control DN. On success, their Control DN for that cast increases by **+4**. This can cause a successful cast to become a Partial Success or Backlash.

## Disruptive Channel
**Prerequisites:** Mana Disruption.  
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Your Mana Disruption can now target:
- **Any spell being cast** (not just Flux)
- **Any spell already active** (weaken its structure — spend an Action, make the opposed Test, and on success reduce its duration by 1 round or reduce its effect by half)

## Spell Contest
**Prerequisites:** Disruptive Channel.  
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
When you successfully disrupt a Flux spell using Mana Disruption, you may choose to enter a **Spell Contest** instead of merely increasing its Control DN.

Make an opposed Insight Test against the caster:
- **You win:** You seize control of the spell. It resolves immediately using your chosen target and variables (within the spell's existing Enhancement limits).
- **You lose:** The spell resolves normally against you, and you take a -2 priority to your next Action as mana feedback rattles your concentration.
