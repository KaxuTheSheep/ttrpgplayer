# Duelist
![[Duelist.png]]
## Sure Parry
**Prerequisites:** None. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+1** to your [[Parry]] Test.

## Confident Parry
**Prerequisites:** Sure Parry.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
The bonus from Sure Parry increases to **+2** total.

## Unbroken Rhythm
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points. 
Your **second** Parry attempt in a [[Rounds|Round]] is no longer made at Disadvantage from [[Parry#Successive Parries|Successive Parries]]. The Disadvantage now begins on your third attempt instead.

## Flowing Defence
**Prerequisites:** Unbroken Rhythm.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your **third** Parry attempt in a [[Rounds|Round]] is also no longer made at Disadvantage. The Disadvantage now begins on your fourth attempt instead.

## Counter Flow
**Prerequisites:** None. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When you choose to make a counter-[[Attacks|attack]] after a successful Parry, your [[Parry]] stance does not end — you may continue Parrying for the remainder of the [[Rounds|Round]] as normal, subject to Successive Parries.

## Read the Strike
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
If you have not yet acted this [[Rounds|Round]], your first Parry Test this Round is made with **Advantage**.

## Answering Blow
**Prerequisites:** Counter Flow.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Your counter-[[Attacks|attack]] after a successful Parry deals **+1** [[Damage Types|damage]] die of your [[Weapon|weapon]]'s type.

## Fluid Guard
**Prerequisites:** Sure Parry, 3 [[Attributes|Grace]].
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points. 
You no longer suffer Disadvantage on a Parry Test from a lower [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] Parry compared to the incoming [[Attacks|attack]]'s [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]].

## Blade Dancer
**Prerequisites:** Confident Parry, 4 [[Attributes|Grace]]. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You score a Critical Success on a Parry Test on a natural roll of **9 or 10**, instead of only a natural 10.

## Weightless Step
**Prerequisites:** Read the Strike, 4 [[Attributes|Grace]].
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
When you fail a Parry Test due to a failed [[Attributes|Grace]] Test (not due to [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]]), you may spend your Reaction to reduce the [[Damage Types|damage]] die increase from **+2 to +1**, provided you have not already used your Reaction this [[Rounds|Round]].

## Perfect Deflection
**Prerequisites:** Fluid Guard, 5 [[Attributes|Grace]]. 
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points. 
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], a failed Parry Test may instead be treated as a success, provided the margin of failure was **3 or less**. This does not apply to Critical Failures.

## Reactive Parry
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You may use [[Parry]] as a **Reaction** even if you did not declare it during the Declaration Phase, functioning like Reactive [[Dodge]]'s alternate use — usable when targeted by a melee [[Attacks|attack]] requiring a roll. It resolves as a standard Parry Test but grants **no** counter-[[Attacks|attack]] option, and ends immediately after resolving the triggering [[Attacks|attack]]. This does not require you to have declared Parry stance, but still consumes your Reaction for the [[Rounds|Round]].

## Snap Deflection
**Prerequisites:** Reactive Parry, 4 [[Attributes|Grace]]. 
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], you may use Reactive Parry even if you have already used your Reaction this Round, provided this is the first time you've done so this Round.

## Perpetual Guard
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Flowing Defence, Answering Blow, Blade Dancer, or Snap Deflection. 
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
You may declare [[Parry]] stance as normal **and** still use Reactive Parry against a different attacker later in the same [[Rounds|Round]], rather than the two being mutually exclusive with your Reaction use. Reactive Parry used this way still consumes your Reaction unless Snap Deflection's condition is met.
