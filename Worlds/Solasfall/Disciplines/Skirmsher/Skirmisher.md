# Skirmisher
![[Skirmisher.png]]
## Dash Strike
**Prerequisites:** None.  
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
You gain access to a new combined action: move up to your Walk Travel Rate, then make one weapon [[Attacks|Attack]]. Both occur within a single [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]] at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] equal to your weapon's Attack Priority **− 2**. This costs a **Full [[Actions|Action]]**.

## Parting Blow
**Prerequisites:** None.  
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
You gain access to a new combined action: make one weapon [[Attacks|Attack]], then move up to your Walk Travel Rate. Both occur within a single [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]] at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] equal to your weapon's Attack Priority **− 2**. This costs a **Full [[Actions|Action]]**.

## Quickstep
**Prerequisites:** None, 2 Grace.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
Gain **+1 Initiation [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]]** to all [[Movement Types]].

## Fleeting Motion
**Prerequisites:** None. 
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
Gain **+0.5m per [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]]** to your Walk and Run Travel Rates.

## Hunter's Stride
**Prerequisites:** Fleeting Motion.  
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
Gain a further **+1m per [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]]** to your Walk Travel Rate specifically.

## Runner's Instinct
**Prerequisites:** Quickstep, 3 Grace.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
Gain a further **+1 Initiation [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]]** to Run specifically.

## Practiced Flow
**Prerequisites:** Dash Strike or Parting Blow.  
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
All Superior Actions you know now resolve at your weapon's Attack Priority **− 1** instead of − 2.

## Full Sprint
**Prerequisites:** Dash Strike.  
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.  
Dash Strike's movement segment now uses your Run Travel Rate instead of Walk.

## Clean Break
**Prerequisites:** Parting Blow.  
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.  
Parting Blow's movement segment ignores difficult terrain and does not provoke a Reach Punish on its own.

## Sweeping Advance
**Prerequisites:** Dash Strike, Parting Blow.  
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.  
You gain access to a new combined action: move up to your Walk Travel Rate, make one weapon [[Attacks|Attack]], then move up to your Walk Travel Rate again. All segments occur within a single [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]] at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] equal to your weapon's Attack Priority **− 2**. This costs a **Full [[Actions|Action]]**.

## Evasive Advance
**Prerequisites:** Sweeping Advance.  
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.  
Any creature attempting a Reach Punish, [[Parry]] counter-attack, or similar punish against you triggered by a movement segment of a Superior Action suffers **Disadvantage** on that roll.

## Storming Assault
**Prerequisites:** Sweeping Advance.  
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.  
You gain access to a new combined action: move up to your Walk Travel Rate, then make **two** weapon [[Attacks|Attacks]]. All segments occur within a single [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]] at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] equal to your weapon's Attack Priority **− 2**. This costs **2 Full [[Actions|Actions]]**.

## Relentless Advance
**Prerequisites:** Storming Assault.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.  
Storming Assault now costs **1 Full [[Actions|Action]]** instead of 2.

## Unbroken Momentum
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Full Sprint, Evasive Advance, Relentless Advance.  
**Cost:** 5 Trait Points.  
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], you may perform a Superior Action you know without paying its [[Actions|Action]] cost, provided it is not the same named Superior Action you have already used this Round.
