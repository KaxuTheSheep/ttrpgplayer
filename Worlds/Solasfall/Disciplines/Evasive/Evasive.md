# Evasive
![[Evasive.png]]
## Light Footwork
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+1 Evasion**.

## Loose Stance
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Your free reposition after triggering Dodge increases from **2m to 4m**.

## Reflexive Guard
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
When you use **Reactive Dodge**, gain **+1** to the Evasion bonus it grants against that [[Attacks|attack]].

## Sure Footing
**Prerequisites:** Light Footwork.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
The bonus from Light Footwork increases to **+2**.

## Rolling Recovery
**Prerequisites:** Loose Stance, 2 Grace.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Your free reposition from Dodge no longer provokes anything and may pass through difficult terrain without penalty or an [[Skills|Athletics]] Test.

## Anticipation
**Prerequisites:** Reflexive Guard.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Once per [[Rounds|Round]], you may use **Reactive Dodge** even if you have already used your Reaction this Round, provided this is the first time you've done so this [[Rounds|Round]].

## Unsettled Guard
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
Gain **+1 Evasion** against the second and subsequent [[Attacks|Attack]] made against you by the same creature in a single [[Rounds|Round]].

## Slipstream
**Prerequisites:** Loose Stance, Rolling Recovery, 4 Grace.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
If your free reposition from Dodge takes you out of a creature's melee threat range entirely, that creature does not gain a Reach Punish against you for having done so this [[Rounds|Round]].

## Practiced Evasion
**Prerequisites:** None.
**Cost:** 2 Trait Points.
While Dodge is active, your Attack Tests are only at Disadvantage against the specific creature whose attack triggered it — Attacks against other creatures are unaffected.

## Lingering Guard
**Prerequisites:** Practiced Evasion, 3 Grace.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Dodge's Evasion bonus lasts until actions of [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Tick]] equal to (triggering attack Tick − **4**) have resolved, instead of − 3.

## Compounding Guard
**Prerequisites:** Unsettled Guard.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Unsettled Guard's bonus increases to **+2** and now also applies to the first Attack against you each Round, provided you were also attacked by that same creature the previous Round.

## Vanishing Step
**Prerequisites:** Slipstream, 6 Grace.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Once per Short Rest, when Dodge triggers, you may treat your free reposition as up to your full Walk Travel Rate instead of the normal fixed distance.

## Never Still
**Prerequisites:** Anticipation.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
You may declare Dodge and still use Reactive Dodge later in the same Round against a different attacker, rather than the two being mutually exclusive per the normal [[Defensive Stances]] rules.

## Second Instinct
**Prerequisites:** Never Still, 4 Grace.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
The Anticipation restriction ("once per Round") is removed — you may use Reactive Dodge as many times as you are targeted, so long as you have not already declared and triggered proactive Dodge this Round.

## Sustained Motion
**Prerequisites:** Lingering Guard, 5 Grace.
**Cost:** 3 Trait Points.
Dodge's Evasion bonus window extends by a further **1 Tick step** (total −5 from triggering Attack Tick).

## Untouchable
**Prerequisites:** Vanishing Step, Sustained Motion. **Exclusive to Perfect Evasion.**
**Cost:** 4 Trait Points.
Once per Rest, when Dodge triggers, it does not impose Attack Disadvantage for the remainder of that window.

## Perfect Evasion
**Prerequisites:** Any two of Sure Footing, Compounding Guard, Second Instinct. **Exclusive to Untouchable.**
**Cost:** 5 Trait Points.
While Dodge's Evasion bonus is active, the first [[Attacks|attack]] each Round that would hit you instead automatically misses, provided you have not already benefited from this effect this Round. This does not apply to Critical Successes.
