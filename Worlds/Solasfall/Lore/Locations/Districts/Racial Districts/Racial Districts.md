The racial districts of Solasfall stand as living enclaves of memory and survival. Carved from the city’s layered stone or built upon reclaimed ruin, each district bears the mark of a people trying to preserve what the city threatens to erase. Though united under the Veil’s shadow, these quarters pulse with their own faiths, trades, and tempers — reminders that Solasfall’s peace is built on division as much as cooperation.

While Commoner District sprawls as humanity’s home and heart of daily life, the following districts represent the city’s non-Human inhabitants. Each one tells a story of adaptation — or resistance — to the weight of Solasfall’s order.

- **Cindral District** — A shimmering quarter of molten glass and forge-lit devotion, where endurance and Faith burn side by side.
- **Velkhari District** — Twisting alleys and fractal shrines that hum with Rift energy, its strange geometry unsettling to outsiders.
- **Kenku District** — A layered maze of sound and shadow, alive with chattering speech, scavenged craft, and mimicry turned artform.
- **Murk District** — Submerged canals and misted walkways where amphibious folk whisper through the fog and tend to quiet rituals.
- **Drenek District** — Deep tunnels and resonant halls carved straight into the mountain’s bones, echoing with chants of stone and ancestry.

Though their gates stand open, few wander freely between these places. To walk a racial district as an outsider is to glimpse the city’s unspoken boundaries — the line between coexistence and segregation, community and containment.
