Where coin flows like water, and power whispers behind every stall. The Market District pulses at the heart of Solasfall’s economy, filled with merchant stalls, enchanted forges, and hidden alcoves. Rare Hollowdeep relics, alchemical goods, and magical curiosities change hands here daily, watched over by guilds, clergy, and shadowy figures alike.
Trade, contracts, and subtle political influence intermingle with the chaos of haggling voices and foot traffic, making this district both a place of opportunity and danger.
- **Key Features:** Merchant Guild oversight, artisan workshops, Hollowdeep trade, and a lively black market.
- **Culture:** Negotiation, bargaining, and wealth display dominate, with intrigue lurking behind polite smiles.
- **Notable Locations:** The Ledger Hall, secretive auction houses, and guild-controlled trading halls.
