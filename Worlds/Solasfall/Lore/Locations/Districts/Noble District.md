Power carved in stone; faith draped in gold. The Noble District crowns Solasfall, home to ancient bloodlines, towering estates, and the grandest cathedrals. Wealth here is measured not just in coin, but in legacy, influence, and the favour of Deities and advisors alike.

Nobles walk marbled streets lined with guarded estates, private shrines, and secret passages. Politics, worship, and rivalry intertwine at every turn, while the Knights Order maintains a constant presence to protect both the city’s elite and its secrets.

- **Key Features:** Walled family estates, grand cathedrals, and exclusive market connections.
- **Culture:** Courts, clergy sponsorships, and subtle intrigues dominate daily life.
- **Notable Locations:** House Cindelreth’s Estate, the Grand Reliquary of The Blind Shepherd, and the Veil Garden.
