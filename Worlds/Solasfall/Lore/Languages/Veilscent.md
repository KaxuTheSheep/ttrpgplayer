Veilscent is a pheromonal language that the parasite use the communicate between members of their own species, due to this biological nature only velkhari possess the ability to communicate in this language.
### Smell Profile 
Veilscent communicates entirely through pheromone emission patterns rather than sound. Meaning is carried through specific biochemical signatures released into the surrounding air, perceived instinctively by other Velkhari as structured “semantic scent”.

The system is highly organic and metabolically linked: emotional state, intent, and emphasis all modulate the composition and intensity of emissions. Communication is continuous rather than discrete, allowing layered or overlapping messages in a shared space.

#### Primary scent lexicon
- **Wet stone / mineral dampness** → neutral statements, factual transmission
- **Fresh rain on earth (petrichor)** → acknowledgement, presence, simple affirmation
- **Cold metal / iron trace** → conflict, challenge, dominance signalling
- **Ozone-like sharpness** → alert, urgency, attention shift
- **Moss and shaded growth** → trust, familiarity, social bonding
- **Fungal earth / rich decay-organic notes** → group cohesion, shared intent, collective [[Actions|action]]
- **Saltwater brine** → inquiry, uncertainty, probing intent
- **Resinous sap / warm plant exudate** → commitment, promise, resolve
- **Ash-like dryness (organic combustion residue)** → loss, grief, closure
- **Bitter green compounds** → rejection, warning, refusal

#### Structural modifiers
Meaning is altered through:

- **Intensity gradient** (whispered trace vs saturated presence)
- **Temporal decay** (rapid fade vs sustained emission)
- **Layering** (simultaneous emission of multiple scent meanings)
- **Spatial directionality** (source-focused vs diffuse ambient release)

#### Emotional bleed
Because communication is biologically generated, emotional state naturally interferes unless actively regulated:

- stress → metallic/acidic contamination of baseline scent
- aggression → iron and ozone amplification
- fear → sulphuric mineral sharpness and damp cave undertones
- attraction/trust → warm resin and moss dominance

#### Perceptual constraint
Only Velkhari possess the neural and sensory structures to parse Veilscent as language. To non-Velkhari, it is experienced as shifting atmospheric smell patterns with no stable linguistic segmentation, making conversations effectively invisible.
