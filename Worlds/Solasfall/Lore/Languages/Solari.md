Solari is the native tongue of Humans, rich in dialects but standardized enough to serve as the language of nobility and scholarly pursuit. It has a formal written script used in governmental, religious, and arcane texts. Solari’s complexity reflects human society’s layered hierarchies and customs.
### Sound Profile
Solari is structured, highly articulated, and moderately formal in speech. It tends toward clear syllable separation with controlled stress patterns, allowing meaning to remain stable across dialects while still permitting regional variation.

Phonetically, it supports a broad vowel inventory and a wide range of consonants, but avoids excessive reduction or elision in formal registers. In educated or ceremonial use, speech becomes more measured and deliberate, with emphasis placed on cadence and clarity rather than [[Speed & Resolution|speed]].

Dialectal forms can vary significantly in rhythm and softness, but the standardized Solari used in official, scholarly, and religious contexts prioritises precision, intelligibility, and rhetorical flow over colloquial efficiency.
