Gilded Mints are the official currency of Solasfall, pegged to the value of Mana Stones. While the full exchange process is managed by The Ministry of Balance and standardised Mana Stones, the Mints themselves circulate as coinage for everyday trade, commerce, and services. Their value is recognised citywide, with each Mint representing a fixed quantity of mana.
