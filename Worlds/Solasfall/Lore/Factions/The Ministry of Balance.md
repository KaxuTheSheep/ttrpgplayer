### Overview
The Ministry of Balance is the unseen hand that controls [[Solasfall]]'s economy — regulating the city's trade in [[Mana]] Stones, enforcing taxation, and ensuring the flow of magical energy required to sustain the [[Veil]]. Its authority derives not from armies or sorcery, but from its unyielding grip on resources.

See also: Gilded Mints, [[Mana]] Stones, and The Weighing under Economy.

### Purpose & History
Formed shortly after the [[Veil]]'s creation to stabilise the chaotic trade of [[Mana]] Stones, the Ministry introduced Gilded Mints — a currency magically pegged to the value of stones harvested from the [[Hollowdeep]]. This replaced the unreliable physical weighing of [[Mana|mana]] and created a rigid economic order.

### Core Responsibilities
- **Pegging the Currency:** The Ministry continually recalculates the exchange rate between [[Mana]] Stones and Gilded Mints at verified assay points across the city.
- **Taxation and Enforcement:** Every citizen must pay their due in Gilded Mints during the Weighing, on pain of death or forced conscription into [[Hollowdeep]] salvage teams.
- **The Weighing:** Occurring two weeks after the [[Rift]] opens, this ritual audits the city's wealth and punishes those who fail to maintain their share of the "balance." See The Weighing for full detail.

### Public Perception
To most, the Ministry is a heartless, inevitable force. Merchants grudgingly respect the stability it brings, while explorers and common folk alike fear its reach. Rumours circulate of secret vaults brimming with hoarded [[Mana|mana]] and forbidden knowledge, deepening suspicion and unease.

### Symbol & Motto
A set of scales forged from gold and stone, forever tipped unevenly to remind citizens of their precarious place in the grand equation.
_"Balance is Survival."_
