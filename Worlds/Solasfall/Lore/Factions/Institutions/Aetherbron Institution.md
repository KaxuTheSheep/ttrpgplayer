*Specialists in direct force application and [[Mana|mana]] sensing — the manipulation of kinetic energy, pressure, and gravity, and the use of [[Mana|mana]] as an environmental sensing medium.*

## Overview
The Aetherbron Institution studies the most direct expressions of [[Mana|mana]] manipulation — applying force to matter without an intermediary process, and using [[Mana|mana]] itself as a medium for environmental perception. Where other institutions work through combustion, biology, or light, Aetherbron works on matter and space directly. A barrier is compressed force increasing resistance in a localised area. A [[Magic|magic]] missile is accelerated kinetic energy directed outward. Gravity manipulation is sustained omnidirectional force applied to matter.

Their sensing discipline produces the city's most capable navigators and makes Aetherbron mages naturally suited to Wayfinder roles in [[Hollowdeep]] expeditions, though the role is not exclusive to them.

## Discipline

### Direct Force Application
Aetherbron's most immediately understood capability. [[Mana]] increasing kinetic energy in a defined direction produces force — a [[Magic|magic]] missile is this principle at its simplest. The same principle at larger scale produces area force effects, shockwaves, and sustained pressure constructs.

Barriers are the inverse — [[Mana|mana]] increasing resistance and density in a localised space until matter cannot pass through it cleanly. The strength of the barrier scales with the [[Mana|mana]] sustaining it and collapses when that [[Mana|mana]] is withdrawn or overcome.

### Gravity Manipulation
Aetherbron mages can increase or decrease gravitational pull in a defined area — making objects or creatures heavier, lighter, or redirecting the direction gravity acts on them entirely. This is among the more demanding applications of the discipline, requiring sustained precise control. Practical applications range from combat to construction to [[Hollowdeep]] navigation in nexuses where gravity behaves anomalously.

### [[Mana]] Sensing
Aetherbron's second major discipline. A mage releases a pulse of [[Mana|mana]] into the ambient current and reads the return — the wave encodes information about physical objects and spatial geometry it passes through, functioning as a form of echolocation through the [[Mana|mana]] environment.

[[Mana]] sensing is active rather than passive and reads physical environment only. It provides no information about souls, intentions, or magical effects beyond what those effects have done to the physical space around them. This capability is the foundation of the Wayfinder role. A Wayfinder is any sufficiently trained navigator using [[Mana|mana]] sensing to guide expeditions through unknown terrain — the role is accessible to others through the Pathfinder's Watch, a magical tool that replicates the sensing pulse mechanically for those without conducting ability.

## Role in [[Solasfall]]
Aetherbron's combat utility is significant — direct force application is immediately practical in military and security contexts, and the institution supplies combat-capable mages to the Knights Orders and expedition parties. Their barrier work contributes to city security infrastructure.

Their navigational output through Wayfinder training is equally valued. [[Hollowdeep]] expeditions with Aetherbron-trained members have measurably better survival and return rates.

## Relationships with Other Institutions
Closest practical relationship with the **Explorers Guild** through Wayfinder deployment and expedition support. Collaborate with **Riftconjurors** on spatial applications where force and dimensional boundary work intersect. Work with **Runeweavers** on barrier and force applications encoded into [[Magic|magic]] tools.

## Culture & Reputation
Aetherbron is regarded as reliable and versatile — not the most dramatic institution, not the most theoretically prestigious, but consistently useful across a wider range of practical situations than most. Explorers regard them highly. The Knights Orders value them without fully understanding what they do.

Internally the culture is disciplined and empirical. Students develop patience alongside technical precision.

## Headmaster
**Archmagus Tyren Holl** leads the institution.
