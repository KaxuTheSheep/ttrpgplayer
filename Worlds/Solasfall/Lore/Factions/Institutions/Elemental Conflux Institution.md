*Specialists in environmental process manipulation — the acceleration and suppression of physical processes in air, water, earth, fire, and lightning, and their natural subtypes.*

## Overview
The Elemental Conflux Institution studies the manipulation of large-scale physical and environmental processes through [[Mana|mana]]. Fire, water, earth, air, and lightning are the institution's five founding categories — a framework inherited from the earliest systematic mages who mastered these disciplines.

## Discipline
Conflux [[Magic|magic]] manipulates environmental and physical processes at scale. The underlying mechanism is consistent across all five categories — [[Mana|mana]] increasing or decreasing the rate of a process already present in the environment.

### Fire
Combustion acceleration. Increasing the oxidation rate and thermal energy of existing fuel until ignition occurs, then sustaining or expanding the reaction.

### Air
Atmospheric pressure and kinetic energy manipulation. **Sound** is a natural subtype of air manipulation.

### Water
Manipulation of liquid state matter and phase transition processes. **Ice and mist** are natural subtypes.

### Earth
Pressure, density, and molecular cohesion manipulation in solid matter. **Lava** falls under earth rather than fire, and **material property manipulation** extends earth principles to crafted materials.

### Lightning
Electrical discharge acceleration. The most precise and potentially dangerous of the five categories.

### Weather
Large-scale atmospheric manipulation drawing on air, water, and thermal principles simultaneously — the institution's most complex and demanding discipline.

## Role in [[Solasfall]]
The Conflux serves [[Solasfall]] in combat, infrastructure, and environmental regulation. The Knights Orders deploy them as battlefield support and the Explorers Guild values them highly for [[Hollowdeep]] expeditions.

Temperature regulation, atmospheric management, and the sustained weather operations within the [[Veil]]'s boundary are largely Conflux responsibilities.

## Relationships with Other Institutions
Operationally closest to **Lifebinders** in agricultural and ecological maintenance. Competitive tension with **Runeweavers** over the [[Magic|magic]] tools market. Unique long-term relationship with the **Royal Mages** through the sky layer pipeline.

## Culture & Reputation
The Conflux is the most publicly visible institution. Their [[Magic|magic]] is dramatic, immediate, and comprehensible to anyone who has ever seen fire or felt wind — which makes them both admired and somewhat condescended to by more theoretically oriented institutions.

Internally the culture values control over raw power. Students sort by elemental affinity early and develop deep competency in one category before broadening.

## Headmaster
**Archmagus Ignar Flareborn** leads the institution.
