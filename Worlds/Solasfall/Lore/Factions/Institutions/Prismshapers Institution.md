*Specialists in light manipulation — the bending, suppression, amplification, and redirection of light as a physical phenomenon.*

## Overview
The Prismshapers Institution studies the physical manipulation of light through [[Mana|mana]]. Wavelength, amplitude, and polarisation are the properties they work with — adjusting what light does, where it goes, and what it carries to the eye. Their effects are not illusions in the traditional sense of fabricated falsehoods. They are physically real alterations to light itself.

The institution operates the day/night cycle and atmospheric light conditions within the [[Veil]]'s sky layer, making them as essential to [[Solasfall]]'s daily function as any institution in the city — a fact their quiet reputation rarely reflects.

## Discipline

### Light Suppression
Decreasing light amplitude in a defined area until none reaches the eye produces genuine darkness — not the appearance of darkness, but the actual absence of light. A Prismshaper can suppress light around a specific object or person, rendering them effectively invisible without touching them directly.

### Wavelength Manipulation
Shifting the wavelength of light changes its colour. At extreme adjustment light shifts outside the visible spectrum entirely, becoming invisible to normal eyes but present and detectable by those with appropriate sensitivity or instruments.

### Refraction and Redirection
Bending light around objects or through controlled angles allows Prismshapers to make things appear where they are not, conceal things behind apparent empty space, or create the visual impression of an environment that does not match physical reality.

### Amplification
Increasing light amplitude produces brightness, blinding flashes, and concentrated beams. Combat applications are the most obvious, but amplification also serves signalling and illumination of deep [[Hollowdeep]] nexuses.

### Sky Layer Operations
The [[Veil]]'s sky layer — the sustained light manipulation field producing [[Solasfall]]'s apparent sky, weather light, and day/night cycle — is primarily a Prismshaper operation. Senior practitioners rotate through sky layer duty as a standard part of institutional advancement.

## Role in [[Solasfall]]
Beyond sky layer operations the Prismshapers provide covert support for Royal Mage operations, concealment and misdirection for sensitive expeditions, and security applications across the city's most politically sensitive locations. Their work is by nature invisible when effective.

They also support [[Hollowdeep]] expeditions — concealing camps, managing light in nexus environments where natural illumination is absent or hostile.

## Relationships with Other Institutions
The boundary with **Elemental Conflux** is clearly drawn — atmospheric light is Prismshaper territory, atmospheric matter is Conflux territory. Work regularly with **Royal Mages** through sky layer rotation.

## Culture & Reputation
The Prismshapers are regarded with a mixture of unease and respect. Citizens benefit from their sky layer work daily without thinking about it, but awareness of their concealment capabilities produces quiet wariness.

Internally the culture emphasises precision and restraint. Students are taught that the best Prismshaper work leaves no trace of intervention.

## Headmaster
**Archmagus Corvin Duskveil** leads the institution.
