### Overview
The Cultists are Solasfall's forbidden congregations — secret sects devoted to the three Forbidden Deities: The Maw Below, The Red Thorn, and The Hollow Shade. Unlike the Sanctums, they hold no legal standing, no public shrines, and no protection from the law. Discovery means persecution, imprisonment, or death. Despite this, worship persists in hidden rooms, abandoned tunnels, and quiet gatherings across every district.

### Structure & Organisation
Cult cells are small, secretive, and compartmentalised — exposure is fatal, so cells rarely know of each other even within worship of the same deity. There is no citywide unifying structure for "the Cultists" as a whole; the term is a catch-all used by outsiders for a phenomenon that is really dozens of unconnected, unaffiliated congregations.

### The Three Cults
- **The Maw Below Cult** — Worship of hunger, obsession, and insatiable desire. Shrines are hidden underground, in ruins, or beneath the city.
- **The Red Thorn Cult** — Worship of forbidden love, secrets, and memory manipulation. Operates through quiet infiltration of social and personal networks rather than fixed shrines.
- **The Hollow Shade Cult** — Worship of shadows, lost souls, and forgotten truths. Operates from hidden rooms, abandoned tunnels, and secret societies.

### Public Perception
Ordinary citizens fear and avoid confirmed cult activity, though rumour and superstition about "what really happens" in the city's forgotten spaces run far ahead of any confirmed fact. Most citizens have never knowingly met a cultist and would not recognise one if they had.
