### Overview
The Royal Mages are [[Solasfall]]'s elite corps of magical experts, tasked with maintaining the [[Veil]], overseeing Riftconjurors Institution operations, and enforcing magical law when needed. Serving the King and the Royal Advisors, they maintain a degree of autonomy in both research and tactical deployment, blending scholarly pursuit with disciplined combat capability.

### Role & Influence
- Responsible for sustaining the [[Veil]]'s [[Mana|mana]], ensuring citywide stability and environmental control.
- Supervise [[Rift|rift]] operations and [[Hollowdeep]] expeditions in collaboration with the Explorers' Guild and Riftconjurors Institution.
- Serve as a magical enforcement arm for the Knights Orders, occasionally intervening in covert Rebel Group or criminal activities.
- Conduct research into advanced [[Mana|mana]] manipulation, spellcraft, and experimental applications beyond institutional limits.

### Culture and Expectations
- Highly disciplined, valuing precision, efficiency, and loyalty.
- Members cultivate both combat skill and magical expertise; research and fieldwork are both considered essential.
- Publicly, they are professionals devoted to the [[Veil]] and city safety.

### Public Perception
- Feared for their combat ability and the flexibility to use [[Magic|magic]] offensively with legal immunity.
- Respected for their role in protecting [[Solasfall]] from the Fallows and ensuring safe exploration of the [[Hollowdeep]].
- Mysterious due to their secretive operations, arcane experiments, and partial absence from public life.
