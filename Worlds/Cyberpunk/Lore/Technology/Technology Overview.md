# Technology Overview

*What is actually different in 2100. The physical and engineering reality underneath the world.*

---

## Design Philosophy

Technology in this world follows two principles simultaneously:

**Uneven advancement** — technologies that generate conglomerate profit advance rapidly. Technologies that would benefit populations without a clear profit mechanism stagnate or get actively deprioritised. The result is a world that is extraordinary in some dimensions and neglected in others simultaneously.

**Grounded extrapolation** — nothing in this world requires new physics. Every technology is an extrapolation of real research directions that exist today, given 75 years of development time and corporate rather than state investment priorities.
