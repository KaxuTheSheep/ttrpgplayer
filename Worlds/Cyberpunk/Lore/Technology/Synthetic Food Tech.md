# Synthetic Food Tech

*How [[Synthetic Food]] is made. What it can and cannot do.*

---

## The Technology

Synthetic food production is an industrialised manufacturing process — caloric content is achievable at scale and cheaply. Carbohydrate, fat, and protein equivalents are synthesised from base chemical processes rather than agricultural inputs. The result is shelf-stable, calorie-accurate, and nutritionally incomplete.

The gap is micronutrients — vitamins, minerals, and trace compounds that current synthetic processes either cannot replicate efficiently or cannot replicate at all at commercial scale. This is not a secret or a conspiracy; it is a known technical limitation that nobody in the Federal States has sufficient incentive to solve, because solving it would reduce dependency on the product and because [[GMO Agricultural Imports]] already serve the market that can pay for nutrition.

---

## Scale and Cost

Fully scaled. Synthetic food is the cheapest and most available calorie source in New Halcyon. Conglomerate-owned production facilities operate at city-feeding capacity. The product exists at multiple quality and price tiers — all of them micronutrient deficient, some more palatably disguised than others.

---

## The Micronutrient Problem

Long-term exclusive consumption produces progressive deficiency conditions: weakened immunity, cognitive effects, bone deterioration, reduced life expectancy. These effects are slow enough that they feel like ordinary ill health rather than a single identifiable cause — which is useful, from a liability perspective, for the conglomerates that produce and sell the food.

See [[Micronutrient Research]] for the Federal States' current (inadequate) response.
