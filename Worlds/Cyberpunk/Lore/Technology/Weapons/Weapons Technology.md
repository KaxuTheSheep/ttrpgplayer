# Weapons Technology

*No regulatory ceiling. What conglomerates find profitable to sell and what they keep exclusive to their own forces.*

---

## The Regulatory Vacuum

Weapons in New Halcyon exist in the same market logic as everything else — manufactured by conglomerates, sold at whatever tier the market will bear, with no external authority capable of setting limits on what civilians can acquire. Full automatic weapons are standard civilian purchases. The distinctions that exist are economic and commercial rather than legal.

Each conglomerate's weapons manufacturing subsidiary makes different decisions about what to sell to whom. One may cap civilian sales at automatic rifles because the liability and reputational calculus favours it. Another may sell technology that looks alien compared to present-day firearms because the premium market for advanced hardware justifies the development cost. Neither decision is regulated from outside.

Military-grade hardware and civilian-grade hardware are not legally distinct categories. They are price points and distribution channels. A well-funded militia and a conglomerate Tier 1 unit may be running equivalent hardware because nothing prevents it.

## Baseline — Refined Conventional Firearms

By 2100 conventional firearms are recognisable but refined to a degree that makes current weapons look crude.

**Caseless ammunition** is the most significant mainstream change. No cartridge case means less weight per [[Rounds|round]], higher capacity for the same magazine volume, no ejection mechanism which removes moving parts and failure points, and faster theoretical fire rates without case extraction limiting the cycle. The [[Rounds|round]] is propellant and projectile as a single unit. This technology is in development today; by 2100 it is the standard across most [[Weapon|weapon]] categories.

**Electronic firing mechanisms** replace mechanical ones in mid to high tier weapons. The trigger sends a signal rather than physically striking a primer. This enables programmable fire rates, biometric authentication in the grip, shot counters, remote diagnostics, and integration with the network pairing protocol. A [[Weapon|weapon]] that recognises its authorised user and refuses to fire for anyone else is a standard product feature rather than a premium one.

**Active recoil compensation** through gyroscopic stabilisers, electronically timed muzzle devices, and stock systems that actively push against recoil rather than absorbing it passively. Full automatic fire at meaningful calibres becomes genuinely controllable.

**Materials** — graphene and ceramic composites make weapons significantly lighter than current equivalents without sacrificing structural integrity. A full automatic rifle in 2100 weighs less than many current pistols.

## Network Integration

Weapons with network cards are a distinct category from those without. The pairing protocol connects a networked [[Weapon|weapon]] to the shooter's augment ecosystem.

**Augmented eye and [[Weapon|weapon]] pairing** — the [[Weapon|weapon]]'s sensor array and the shooter's augmented vision share targeting data. The [[Weapon|weapon]] knows where the user is looking; the user's augmented vision receives [[Weapon|weapon]] status, ammunition count, and targeting assistance. Aiming becomes a conversation between two paired systems rather than a mechanical alignment of sights. The accuracy ceiling for an augmented shooter with paired hardware exceeds what either component achieves alone.

**The vulnerability** — a networked [[Weapon|weapon]] accepts connections through the pairing protocol and is therefore subject to the same injection attack vectors as any other paired device. A [[Weapon|weapon]] that will not fire when needed because a malfunction signal has been pushed through its network card is a specific and potentially fatal failure mode.

**The response** — serious combat operators increasingly run weapons with no network card. A dumb [[Weapon|weapon]] that executes exactly what the trigger says and nothing else is a valued feature in an environment where smart weapons can be interfered with. This creates a market split: networked weapons for general sale, deliberately unnetworked weapons at a premium for operators who understand the vulnerability. Knowledge and money buying different things again.

## Railguns

Electromagnetic acceleration of a conductive projectile — not science fiction by 2100, an engineering solution to the power density problem that [[Quantum & Solid State Batteries|quantum and solid state batteries]] make viable in a handheld form factor.

The projectile is a graphene composite flechette — graphene's extraordinary conductivity makes it more effective as a railgun projectile than steel, and its strength-to-weight ratio means a small projectile carries significant kinetic energy at hypersonic velocity. The wound profile is unlike conventional firearms — a narrow, deep penetration channel created by a projectile moving far faster than any conventional [[Rounds|round]].

**Characteristics:**
- No propellant — no muzzle flash, no combustion signature, no powder residue
- The sound is the hypersonic crack of the projectile breaking the sound barrier after leaving the barrel — loud but directionally confusing, seeming to originate from the projectile's path rather than the shooter's position
- No recoil in the conventional sense — acceleration happens along the entire barrel rather than instantaneously
- High instantaneous power draw — quantum batteries handle this; solid state batteries at compact sizes do not have the discharge rate for sustained use, making handheld railguns either quantum battery dependent or limited to single shots with charge intervals

## Directed Energy

Laser and particle beam weapons viable at handheld scale by 2100 — the power density problem that makes them impractical today is an engineering problem the intervening decades solve.

**Characteristics:**
- Speed of light travel time — no leading a moving target
- No ballistic drop
- Visible beam path in atmosphere — immediately reveals shooter position
- Thermal bloom in the emitter under sustained fire — cannot fire indefinitely without cooling
- Power dependent — only as capable as the battery driving it

Directed energy weapons are not universally superior to conventional firearms. The beam visibility is a significant tactical disadvantage in situations where revealing position is fatal. They occupy a specific role rather than replacing conventional weapons.

## EMP Weapons

The most contextually specific [[Weapon|weapon]] in New Halcyon because its lethality is a function of the target's augmentation level rather than its physical characteristics.

A concentrated EMP [[Weapon|weapon]] delivers a focused electromagnetic pulse to a specific target rather than area effect. Against an unaugmented person it is a non-lethal harassment tool at best. Against a lightly augmented person it disrupts systems without necessarily being fatal. Against a heavily augmented person — particularly one running [[Quantum & Solid State Batteries|quantum batteries]] whose coherence is disrupted — it is a clean kill.

**Why EMP kills heavily augmented targets:** augments replace or supplement biological functions. A heart augment that stops is cardiac arrest. An NI going down mid-signal is potentially a seizure. An MPU losing power when biological functions depend on it is cascading organ failure. The more augmented the target, the more biological function depends on hardware that the EMP disables.

**The concealment value:** from the outside, a concentrated EMP kill looks like catastrophic augment failure, which happens. Distinguishing a precision EMP kill from genuine system failure requires forensic capability that only exists at the conglomerate level and is only applied when the victim matters enough to warrant it. The [[Weapon|weapon]] has obvious implications for assassination and deniable operations.

**Quantum battery vulnerability:** targets running quantum batteries are more susceptible to EMP than those running solid state, because quantum coherence disruption releases stored energy unpredictably in addition to cutting power. The best-equipped targets are the most vulnerable to this specific [[Weapon|weapon]] type.

## The Dumb [[Weapon]] Premium

Across all [[Weapon|weapon]] categories a pattern emerges: the most sophisticated weapons are networked, smart, and integrated with the augment ecosystem. The most operationally secure weapons are deliberately dumb — no network card, no electronic firing mechanism, no connected systems.

A graphene-composite caseless automatic rifle with mechanical electronic ignition and no network capability costs more than an equivalent networked [[Weapon|weapon]] because the market for it is smaller and the manufacturing is slightly more complex without the electronic components. But it cannot be interfered with remotely, cannot be authenticated against the shooter, and has no attack surface for injection attacks.

Greyback Company and experienced independent operators tend toward this end of the market. Understanding why is one of the markers that distinguishes serious combat experience from consumer augment ownership.
