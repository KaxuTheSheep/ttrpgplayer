Blessings are tangible gifts from the Deities, granting followers divine boons that enhance abilities, provide protections, or offer unique advantages. While each deity’s blessings are unique, the following rules apply to all blessings in [[Solasfall]].
### Duration and Renewal
- Blessings last for **one week** of in-game time.
- Renewal requires **prayer, ritual, or continued [[faith]]**.
- Failure to renew causes the blessing’s effects to **fade** until it is restored.
### Activation and Use
- Most blessings function **passively**, granting continuous benefits.
- Some blessings may include **active abilities**, require **uses per day**, or need a **long [[Rest & Recovery|rest]]** to recharge.
- Specific usage rules are detailed for each blessing.
### Stacking and Limits
- Characters may receive blessings from **multiple Deities simultaneously**.
- Only the **highest-tier blessing** from a single deity applies; multiple blessings from the same Deities do **not stack**.
- Blessings from **different Deities** do stack.
### Charges and Usage
- Some blessings include **limited charges or uses**, reflecting the Deity’s cautious bestowal of power.
- Charges typically **replenish** according to the blessing’s description, often daily or after a long [[Rest & Recovery|rest]].
### Curses and Consequences
- Blessings from **Forbidden Deities** are paired with **curses**, introducing risks or drawbacks.
- Curses may manifest as **mechanical penalties, social stigma, or supernatural flaws**.
- Partial [[faith]] in a Forbidden Deities may result in **minor curses** without full consequences.
