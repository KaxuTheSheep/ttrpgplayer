Solasfall thrives on networks of influence, authority, and secrecy. Factions range from noble houses and guilds to rebel cells and secret cults. They control resources, enforce rules, or exploit gaps in the city’s complex systems. Understanding factions is key for navigating the city safely—or to gain power oneself.
## Government & Authority
- The Governance of Solasfall – Central administration and the ruling apparatus of the city.
- The Ministry of Balance – Bureaucracy enforcing taxation, law, and control over Hollowdeep expeditions.
- The Noble Faction – Influential families shaping politics and funding religious and civic endeavours.
- The Knights Orders – Militarized enforcers of law and noble interests, maintaining order in the streets.
- The Royal Mages – Elite corps of mages responsible for maintaining the Veil, supervising Riftconjurors Institution operations, supporting The Knights Orders in magical enforcement, and conducting advanced research and Hollowdeep oversight.

## Religion
- The Sanctums – Organized institutions for the Six Main Deities of Solasfall.
- The Cultists – Secret sects devoted to Forbidden Deities, operating in shadows.

## Trade & Profession
- Magic Institutions – Schools and research collectives controlling the arcane.
- The Explorers' Guild – Specialists in Hollowdeep exploration and relic acquisition.
- The Merchant Guild – Oversees trade, marketplaces, and commercial influence.

## Underground & Resistance
- The Rebel Group – Anti-establishment factions opposing taxation and oppressive policies.
- The Thieves' Guild – Operates covertly, trafficking goods, secrets, and influence.
