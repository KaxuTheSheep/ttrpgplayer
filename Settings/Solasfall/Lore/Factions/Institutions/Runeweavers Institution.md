*Specialists in structured mana encoding — the construction, application, and theory of magic circles and the tools built from them.*

## Overview
The Runeweavers Institution specialises in Weave casting and the creation of magic tools. Where most institutions focus on what mana can do, the Runeweavers focus on how to encode those effects into permanent or semi-permanent structures — circles, sigils, and geometric frameworks that execute a defined magical effect when activated. Their work underpins much of Solasfall's magical infrastructure and supplies the city with tools that allow non-mages to access magical effects entirely.

## Discipline
Weave casting constrains mana through physical structure rather than real-time control. A completed circle encodes instructions that mana follows upon activation. All institutions teach Weave casting to some degree. The Runeweavers specialise in it entirely.

### Magic Tools
The institution's most significant practical output is magic tools — objects containing a prepared two-circle system that produces a defined magical effect on activation via a mechanical trigger. Magic tools run on ambient mana rather than the user's soul. No conducting ability is required to operate one.

Magic tools place magical effects in the hands of anyone who can afford them, independent of magical training. The Runeweavers control the production of the most complex and valuable tools, giving them considerable commercial influence.

### Circle Recovery
A secondary but valued function of the institution is the analysis and reproduction of magic circles recovered from the Hollowdeep. Runeweavers accompanying expeditions document and where possible extract these structures, returning them for analysis.

## Role in Solasfall
The Runeweavers supply magical infrastructure across the city — runic wards, lighting systems, mana-conductive conduits, and the enchanted components embedded in civic structures. They collaborate with the Order of the Gilded Chain on runed armour and weapons, and with the Lifebinders on healing tools and expedition equipment.

## Relationships with Other Institutions
Work closely with **Lifebinders** on equipment incorporating biological effects and with the **Order of the Gilded Chain** on military applications. Their relationship with **Elemental Conflux** is competitive in the tools market.

## Culture & Reputation
The Runeweavers are regarded as the most methodical and artisan-oriented of the institutions. Citizens interact with their work constantly without always recognising it. Among other mages they are respected for precision and occasionally condescended to for their lack of raw casting power.

Internally the culture fuses craftsmanship with engineering.

## Headmaster
**Archmagus Oren Valeclay** leads the institution.
