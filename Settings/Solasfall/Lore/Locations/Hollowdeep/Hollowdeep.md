The Hollowdeep is a vast web of interconnected worlds — nexuses, each self-contained, linked to others by stable or unstable spatial rifts. It is not a vertical structure. It is a network, branching outward in every direction from the entry point, with paths leading to further nexuses the deeper explorers venture. Nobody knows how far it extends or how many nexuses exist beyond the mapped ones.

See [[Rift]] for how the Hollowdeep connection works cosmologically and why it is existentially necessary to Solasfall.

## Time

The Hollowdeep's collective time rate averages across all its connected nexuses. The result is approximately seven times Solasfall's — one day in Solasfall equals seven days inside the Hollowdeep once the controlled rift closes. Individual nexuses may have their own time characteristics contributing to that average, but the 7:1 ratio is consistent throughout the web from an explorer's perspective.

While the controlled rift is open, time syncs between Solasfall and the Hollowdeep. The decoupling begins the moment it closes.

## Structure

### The Shattered Expanse
The first nexus and the only one directly connected to Solasfall. All explorers enter here. The Shattered Expanse functions as a staging ground — relatively safer near its centre, increasingly hostile toward its edges where portals to deeper nexuses are located. Explorers entering early in the one-hour window land near the centre. Those entering late are placed closer to the edges, immediately adjacent to deeper and more dangerous territory.

The Shattered Expanse contains portals to four distinct nexuses, each leading into a different branch of the web.

### Deeper Nexuses
Each nexus beyond the Shattered Expanse is a self-contained world — ecologically and thematically distinct, with its own environment, hazards, and inhabitants. They range from corrupted dreamscapes and bioluminescent fungal jungles to gravity-warped chasms and fortresses built from materials that do not exist in Solasfall. Each contains multiple exit portals leading further into the web. Some are stable and open. Others require mana input, ritual components, or solving arcane mechanisms to activate.

Monsters grow more alien and resilient the deeper into the web explorers venture. The ecology of each nexus has adapted entirely to its own conditions with no reference to the surface world.

### Portals
Portals are the connective tissue of the Hollowdeep — permanent spatial rifts between nexuses. Not all are stable. Some malfunction, transporting explorers to unintended nexuses. The web shifts subtly over time, meaning maps from previous expeditions may be partially outdated. Accurate mapping across multiple expeditions is one of the most valuable things the Explorers Guild maintains.

## The Rift Cycle

Every four weeks the Riftconjurors force open the controlled rift to the Shattered Expanse for one hour. During this window explorers enter and time is synced. Once it closes, the 7:1 ratio applies. After exactly seven Hollowdeep days a return portal opens for one hour — the same duration in both directions since it is another rift opening with synced time. Missing this window means waiting twenty-eight Hollowdeep weeks for the next cycle, which is near-certain death for anyone without extraordinary resources.

The four-week cycle is a practical limit of the Riftconjurors' capacity, not a chosen interval.

## Resources and Economy

The Hollowdeep's primary value to Solasfall is Mordant Stones — without a continuous supply the Veil fails and the Fallows consumes the city. Essences and relics are secondary but significant, driving the Explorers Guild's commercial operations and the risk appetite of independent expeditions.

Food, water, and breathable air are finite inside the Hollowdeep. Certain nexuses alter hunger, fatigue, or mana consumption rates. Explorers cannot rely on the surface world's assumptions about how long supplies last.

## Survival Considerations

**Wayfinders** — arcane navigators essential for mapping, sensing portal shifts, and tracking time against the return window. Expeditions without them have a significantly lower survival rate.

**Safe Zones** — rare sanctuaries within nexuses providing brief respite. Their locations are among the most guarded information the Explorers Guild holds.

**Isolation** — being cut off from known portals is effectively a death sentence on a long enough timeline. Exhaustion, hunger, and the psychological pressure of the Hollowdeep's environments compound rapidly. Mental deterioration is as significant a threat as physical danger on extended or stranded expeditions.

**Party Dynamics** — coordinated parties have substantial advantages in combat, resource management, and navigation. Solo explorers face increased danger across every category with no redundancy when things go wrong.

## What Is Known and Unknown

The four nexuses accessible from the Shattered Expanse are mapped to varying degrees. What lies beyond them is partially mapped by the Explorers Guild and largely unknown beyond that. The full extent of the web, the nature of its deepest nexuses, and whether anything in the Hollowdeep is aware of Solasfall's existence are open questions.

The Hollowdeep did not form for Solasfall's benefit. It predates the city's knowledge of it entirely. Whatever logic governs its structure and the creatures within it operates independently of any surface world concern.
