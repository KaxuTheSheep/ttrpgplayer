Solasfall is a dense, stratified city built primarily from stone and metal, with occasional wooden elements reminiscent of pre-industrial Victorian architecture. Buildings interlock and lean upon one another, forming narrow streets and tight alleys, while grander homes and civic structures rise slightly above the rest, asserting their place without dominating the skyline. The city expands outward only where the Barrier permits, its edges defined by protective walls and the faint shimmer of the Veil.

Districts are divided by purpose, class, and magical affiliation, each radiating a distinct rhythm. The noble district gleams with polished masonry, intricate ironwork, and the occasional spire, while the commoner and slum districts are a tangle of interconnected roofs, narrow walkways, and soot-darkened facades. Churches punctuate most quarters, from humble chapels to the grand cathedrals clustered around the noble heart.

From within Solasfall, the Veil appears as a serene, natural sky, perfectly ordinary until approached at the city’s fringes. There, the illusion falters, turning pale and misty, with glimpses of the Fallows beyond — a world of corrupted landscapes and otherworldly hues. Despite its pre-industrial charm, subtle arcane trinkets and mana-powered devices hum through the city, keeping the gears of Solasfall turning and the rifts at bay.

Once the last bastion of civilisation, Solasfall now teeters between Faith and ruin. Mana Stones from Hollowdeep fuel both its survival and its greed, and every street carries whispers of rebellion, devotion, and despair.

#### Districts of Solasfall
- **Noble District** — The seat of wealth and worship, where ancient bloodlines rule behind marble walls and gilded lies.
- **Market District** — The city’s golden artery, where relics, magic, and corruption trade hands beneath the gaze of the Merchant Guild.
- **Human Commoner District** — A hive of labour and hope, where the working class holds the city together through grit and quiet ambition.
- **Entertainment District** — A world of masks, music, and indulgence, where laughter hides rebellion and Faith mingles with sin.
- **Slum District** — The city’s fractured heart, where light flickers in defiance of neglect and the forgotten forge new Deities from ashes.
- **Racial Districts** — Semi-detached quarters for non-Human peoples, ruled by their own chiefs and governed by race's own rules.
