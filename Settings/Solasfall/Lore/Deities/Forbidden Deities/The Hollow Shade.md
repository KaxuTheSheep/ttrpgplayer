**Titles:**  
The Forgotten Lord, Shadow of Truth, Keeper of Lost Souls

**Domains:**  
Shadows, Lost Souls, Forgotten Truths

**Symbols & Iconography:**  
The Hollow Shade is symbolized by a dark shrouded figure, an empty mask, or a black flame extinguishing a candle. Symbols appear in hidden alcoves, etched into walls of abandoned buildings, or painted as subtle glyphs in shadowed corners.

**Mythos:**  
Legends describe The Hollow Shade as the guardian of what the world has abandoned—lost souls, erased memories, and hidden truths. Tales warn of those who delve too deeply into forgotten knowledge or betray their kin, vanishing into shadow as the Shade claims them.

**Influence & Presence:**  
Cultists of The Hollow Shade operate from secrecy and obscurity, often in hidden rooms, abandoned tunnels, or secret societies. Its influence manifests through unseen guidance, whispered secrets, and the manipulation of knowledge, often sowing paranoia or obsession in those who seek forbidden truths.

**Worshippers:**  
Followed by assassins, outcasts, and seekers of forbidden knowledge. See Hollow Shade Cult.
