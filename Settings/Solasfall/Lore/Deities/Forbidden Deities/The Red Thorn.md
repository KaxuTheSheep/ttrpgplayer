**Titles:**  
The Hidden Passion, Lady of Secrets, The Memory Weaver

**Domains:**  
Forbidden Love, Secrets, Memory Manipulation

**Symbols & Iconography:**  
The Red Thorn is often depicted as a twisting thorned vine wrapping around a broken heart, or as a rose with black petals stained with crimson. Symbols appear in hidden notes, carved into secret doors, or subtly integrated into jewelry and personal charms.

**Mythos:**  
Legends say The Red Thorn bends memory and emotion to shape desires and secret truths. She is whispered to rewrite bonds, foster forbidden love, and reveal or conceal information to serve hidden ends. Stories of lost lovers, forgotten promises, and uncanny coincidences are attributed to her subtle influence.

**Influence & Presence:**  
Her influence is quiet, pervasive, and often indirect. Cultists operate in secrecy, manipulating social and personal networks to advance hidden agendas or protect forbidden loves. Her power manifests in the soft distortion of memory, clandestine meetings, and whispered secrets that change the course of events.

**Worshippers:**  
Followed by spies, secret lovers, and those seeking to forget or alter painful memories. See Red Thorn Cult.
