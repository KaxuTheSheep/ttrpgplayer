**Titles:**  
Devourer, The Insatiable, Hunger’s Abyss

**Domains:**  
Hunger, Obsession, Insatiable Desire

**Symbols & Iconography:**  
The Maw Below is often represented as a gaping void filled with writhing shadows or monstrous teeth, sometimes carved into altars or hidden in cult chambers. Symbols include a circular black maw, clawed hands reaching inward, or a jagged spiral suggesting endless consumption.

**Mythos:**  
Legends describe The Maw Below as a primordial force lurking beneath the world, constantly consuming and never satisfied. Tales of lost cities, vanishing travellers, and hoarded treasures are attributed to its ceaseless hunger. It teaches that obsession and desire are natural, yet dangerous when unchecked, reflecting the balance between need and destruction.

**Influence & Presence:**  
Cultists and secretive sects invoke The Maw Below in pursuit of forbidden power, wealth, or knowledge. Shrines are hidden underground, in abandoned ruins, or beneath the city, often marked by blood or devoured offerings. Its presence inspires both awe and fear, emphasizing the peril of unrestrained appetite and obsession.

**Worshippers:**  
Followed by cultists craving power, hoarders, and those lost to greed. See Maw Below Cult.
