# Black Market Biologicals

*United Territories-origin biological components, smuggled into New Halcyon. The exit door for those the UT won't let through officially.*

---

## What They Are

Biological components — grown organs, non-Framework tissue, biotech replacements — produced by the United Territories' biotech infrastructure and moved into New Halcyon outside official trade channels. They are the raw material required to fill the biological gaps left when SOMA augments are removed from a body that has been augmented long enough to depend on them.

Officially the UT controls all export of biological components. Unofficially, enough material moves through grey and black channels to sustain a meaningful market inside New Halcyon.

---

## Who Buys Them

**Prospective defectors who lack UT approval** — individuals who want to emigrate but have been assessed as low-value or high-risk by the UT's managed intake system. Without official biological components they cannot safely de-augment. The black market is their only option short of staying.

**Wealthy FS citizens hedging their position** — maintaining a stockpile of biological insurance. Not necessarily planning to leave; simply keeping the option open. A rational precaution for anyone with resources and foresight in a city as unstable as New Halcyon.

**Medical applications** — biological components have uses beyond emigration. Certain conditions, augment failures, and injury types respond better to biological repair than mechanical replacement. Demand exists independently of any emigration intent.

---

## Who Supplies Them

The supply chain is deliberately opaque. Possible source routes:

- UT agricultural and biotech workers with access to surplus or off-book production
- Corrupt intake system officials processing components that never officially arrived
- [[Continental Freight Authority]] as a possible transit layer — whether this is sanctioned, tolerated, or entirely unofficial on the CFA's part is unknown

No single organisation controls the market. It is fragmented, which keeps prices high and reliability low.

---

## Class Dimension

Black market biologicals are expensive. Meaningfully expensive — not a casual purchase even for comfortable middle-tier residents. The emigration option they enable is therefore a luxury, reinforcing the pattern established elsewhere in New Halcyon: the trap is not equally tight for everyone. Wealth buys options. Poverty closes them.

---

## Risks

- Component quality is unverified — provenance is impossible to confirm without UT documentation that by definition does not exist for black market goods
- Surgical installation requires practitioners willing to work outside any regulatory framework (which, in New Halcyon, is not hard to find, but competence varies)
- Possession and trade exist in a legal grey area that Tier 1 conglomerate forces occasionally act on when it serves their interests — usually not because they care about the trade itself, but because controlling the emigration pipeline is a useful lever
