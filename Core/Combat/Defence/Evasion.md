**Evasion** represents your character's ability to avoid incoming attacks through agility, reflexes, and awareness.

**Calculation:**  
**Evasion Score = Base Evasion + Grace + Other Modifiers**

- **Base Evasion:** Starts at **6**.
- **Grace:** Your Grace Attribute, reflecting your natural nimbleness.
- **Other Modifiers:** Bonuses or penalties from abilities, equipment, conditions, or situational factors.

### Resolving Attacks
When an attack is made against you:  
1. The attacker rolls Attack Tests.  
2. Compare the total to your **Evasion score**.  
   - If the total meets or exceeds your Evasion, the attack hits.  
   - Otherwise, the attack misses.
