**Evasion** represents your character's ability to avoid incoming [[Attacks|attacks]] through agility, reflexes, and awareness.

**Calculation:**  
**Evasion Score = Base Evasion + [[Attributes|Grace]] + Other Modifiers**

- **Base Evasion:** Starts at **8**.
- **[[Attributes|Grace]]:** Your [[Attributes|Grace]] [[Attributes|Attribute]], reflecting your natural nimbleness.
- **Other Modifiers:** Bonuses or penalties from abilities, equipment, [[Conditions|conditions]], or situational factors.

### Resolving [[Attacks]]
When an [[Attacks|attack]] is made against you:  
1. The attacker rolls [[Attacks|Attack]] Tests.  
2. Compare the total to your **Evasion score**.  
   - If the total meets or exceeds your Evasion, the [[Attacks|attack]] hits.  
   - Otherwise, the [[Attacks|attack]] misses.
