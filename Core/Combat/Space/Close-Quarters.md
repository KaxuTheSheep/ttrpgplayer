## Close-Quarters & Engagement States

Melee combat is not only about _whether_ you can reach someone, but **how** your [[Weapon|weapon]] interacts at different distances. The distance between combatants determines **control**, **leverage**, and **defensive options**.

### Core Distances
There are **three engagement ranges**:

| [[Range & Threat\|Range]]            | Description                                                                                                         | Tactical Meaning                                                              |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Outside Threat**                   | Neither combatant can reach                                                                                         | No melee [[Attacks\|attacks]] possible.                                       |
| **Threat [[Range & Threat\|Range]]** | Both combatants can reach each other                                                                                | Neutral combat — normal [[Attacks\|attacks]] and defence apply.               |
| **Close-Quarters (CQ)**              | You are **within the opponent's Natural [[Range & Threat\|Range]]** (typically the same or directly adjacent meter) | [[Weapon]] leverage and manoeuvrability become core advantages/disadvantages. |

### How Close-Quarters Is Determined
If Distance ≤ Opponent's Natural [[Range & Threat|Range]] → You are in Close-Quarters with them.

For humanoids this means **sharing a meter or occupying an adjacent meter within 1 meter of them**.

This can occur even if **your [[Weapon|weapon]]'s reach is longer** — the space is too tight for full leverage.

### Effects of Close-Quarters
Close-Quarters modifies **[[Weapon|weapon]] effectiveness**, not the ability to [[Attacks|attack]].

| [[Weapon]] Type / Trait | Effect in Close-Quarters |
|---|---|
| **Weapons with Close-Quarters** | Gain **Advantage** on melee [[Attacks\|Attack]] Tests in CQ. |
| **Weapons with Reach** | Suffer **Disadvantage** on melee [[Attacks\|Attack]] Tests **and** on [[Parry]] Tests while in CQ. |
| **Standard (neither Reach nor Close-Quarters)** | No inherent bonus or penalty. |

### Examples

| Scenario | Result |
|---|---|
| Dagger user closes to same meter vs Spear | Dagger gains Advantage Close-Quarters), Spear suffers Disadvantage (Reach Dead Zone). |
| Sword vs Sword at 1 meter | Neutral — neither Close-Quarters Reach impacts. |
| Axe user keeps distance at 2 meters vs Knife user | Knife user can [[attacks\|attack]]; must close distance or reposition. |
