Every character has access to unarmed [[Attacks|attacks]] as a default fallback regardless of equipment. Unarmed [[Attacks|attacks]] represent punches, strikes, and basic physical blows. Classes may expand on unarmed combat with additional techniques, stances, or effects built on top of this baseline.

## Unarmed [[Attacks|Attack]]
**[[Damage Types|Damage]] Die:** 1d4 + [[Attributes|Might]] or [[Attributes|Grace]]
**[[Damage Types|Damage]] Type:** Blunt
**[[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] (Base):** 11 + [[Attributes|Might]] or [[Attributes|Grace]]
**[[Range & Threat|Range]]:** +0m
**Handedness:** Free — does not occupy a hand slot
**Weight Class:** Exempt — does not contribute to carrying capacity

## Grappling Bonus
While in the [[Core/Combat/Action System/Grapple#Grappling State|Grappling]] state, unarmed [[Attacks|attacks]] deal **1d4 + 2× [[Attributes|Might]] or [[Attributes|Grace]]** instead of the standard [[Damage Types|damage]]. The doubled [[Attributes|attribute]] must match whichever [[Attributes|attribute]] is being used for the [[Attacks|attack]] roll.
