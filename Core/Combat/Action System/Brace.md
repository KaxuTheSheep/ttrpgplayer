Brace is a defensive [[Actions|action]] that allows a character to reset their guard, recover their posture, and restore lost [[Sturdiness]] mid-combat. It represents a fighter tightening their stance, taking a brief reprieve after a successful deflection, or finding a moment of composure under pressure.

## [[Actions|Action]] Cost
Brace can be performed at two levels depending on how much time you commit.

| Version | [[Actions\|Action]] Cost | [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution\|Priority]] | Recovery |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quick Brace | Half [[Actions\|Action]] | 8 | 1d4 + Vitality ÷ 2 rounded up [[Sturdiness]] |
| Full Brace | Full [[Actions\|Action]] | 8 | 1d4 + Vitality [[Sturdiness]] |

## Timing
Brace recovery resolves at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] 8. If an [[Attacks|attack]] applies [[Damage Types|damage]] to your [[Sturdiness]] before [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] 8 is reached, Brace is cancelled and no recovery occurs. If the [[Attacks|attack]] resolves after [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] 8, the [[Sturdiness]] is recovered first and the incoming [[Damage Types|damage]] is then applied to the restored pool.

[[Damage Types|Damage]] that is fully absorbed by Armour DR before reaching [[Sturdiness]] does not cancel Brace.

## How It Works in Practice
Brace rewards characters who build around their defences. A fighter who successfully zeroes out incoming [[Damage Types|damage]] through [[Block]], Armour, or [[Parry]] can Brace freely. A nimble character who Dodges or Parries incoming [[attacks]] creates the opening needed.

> **Example:** A shield fighter declares Full Brace and [[Block]] in the same [[Rounds|round]]. An [[Attacks|attack]] hits at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] 12 — before BracePrioritylves at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] 8. [[Block]] reduces the [[Damage Types|damage]] to 0 via Armour DR. Since no [[Damage Types|damage]] reached [[Sturdiness]], Brace completes and the fighter recovers 1d4 [[Sturdiness]].

> **Example:** A fighter declares Quick Brace. An [[Attacks|attack]] hits at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] 6 — after Brace recovers at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] 8. The fighter recovers [[Sturdiness]] first, then takes the hit against the restored pool.
