## Drawing and Stowing Weapons
To **draw** or **stow** a [[weapon]] costs **1 Half [[Actions|Action]]**.

You cannot draw a [[weapon]] as a **Reaction**, and you cannot use **[[Parry]]** or **[[Block]]** with a [[weapon]] that is not drawn.

If you **drop** a [[weapon]], it is a **free [[Actions|action]]** (once per [[Rounds|round]]). Dropping does not allow you to immediately draw another [[weapon]] during the same moment; you must still spend the [[Actions|action]] to draw.

If you wish to **switch weapons**:
- **Stow** the current [[weapon]] (Half [[Actions|Action]]), then
- **Draw** the new [[weapon]] (Half [[Actions|Action]]).
