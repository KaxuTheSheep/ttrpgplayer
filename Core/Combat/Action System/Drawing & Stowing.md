## Drawing and Stowing Weapons
To **draw** or **stow** a weapon costs **1 Half Action**.

You cannot draw a weapon as a **Reaction**, and you cannot use **Parry** or **Block** with a weapon that is not drawn.

If you **drop** a weapon, it is a **free action** (once per round). Dropping does not allow you to immediately draw another weapon during the same moment; you must still spend the action to draw.

If you wish to **switch weapons**:
- **Stow** the current weapon (Half Action), then
- **Draw** the new weapon (Half Action).
