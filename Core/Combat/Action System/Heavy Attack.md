## Purpose
A **Heavy [[Attacks|Attack]]** represents a more powerful, committed strike that trades [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] or flexibility for increased [[Damage Types|damage]]. It is ideal for breaking through defences or dealing significant harm to a single target.

## Mechanics
- **[[Actions|Action]] Cost:** 2 Full [[Actions]]
- Perform a melee or ranged [[Attacks|attack]] that deals **Triple [[Damage Types|damage]]**.
- Reduces [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] by by –2.
- Apply any standard modifiers ([[Attributes|Might]], [[weapon]] bonuses, Critical Hits) as usual.
