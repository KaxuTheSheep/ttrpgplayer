## Purpose
A **Heavy Attack** represents a more powerful, committed strike that trades speed or flexibility for increased damage. It is ideal for breaking through defences or dealing significant harm to a single target.

## Mechanics
- **Action Cost:** 2 Full Actions
- Perform a melee or ranged attack that deals **double damage**.
- Reduces Speed by –2.
- Apply any standard modifiers (Might, weapon bonuses, Critical Hits) as usual.
