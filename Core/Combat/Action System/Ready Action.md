2. Spend as many [[Actions|actions]] as the [[Actions|action]] you intend to perform.
3. When triggered, [[Attributes|resolve]] at [[Speed & Resolution|speed]] = trigger [[Speed & Resolution|speed]] – 1 (minimum 1). If trigger [[Speed & Resolution|speed]] > your [[Speed & Resolution|speed]], [[Attributes|resolve]] at original [[Speed & Resolution|speed]].
