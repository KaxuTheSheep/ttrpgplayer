1. Declare the [[Conditions|condition]] that will trigger the [[Actions|action]].
2. Spend as many [[Actions|actions]] as the [[Actions|action]] you intend to perform.
3. When triggered, [[Attributes|resolve]] at [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] = trigger [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] – 1 (minimum 1). If trigger [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] > your [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]], [[Attributes|resolve]] at original [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]].
