Reactions are responses taken during the Resolution Phase, allowing a character to defend, reposition, or interrupt when something happens to them. They are not declared at the start of the [[Rounds|Round]].

- You may take one Reaction per [[Rounds|round]].
- A Reaction can only be used if its [[Conditions|conditions]] are met.
- A Reaction happens at the [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] of the [[Actions|action]] or [[Movement Types|movement]] associated with it, unless stated otherwise.

## Common Triggers
- You are targeted by an [[Attacks|attack]].
- An enemy moves into your melee threat.
- You are affected by an effect that allows defensive timing.
- A ranged projectile is in flight toward you.

| Reaction | Trigger | Effect |
| -------- | ------- | ------ |
| [[Dodge]] (Reaction use) | You are targeted by any [[Attacks|attack]] requiring a roll | Avoid the [[Attacks|attack]] if your [[Evasion]] is not overcome |
| Reach Punish | An enemy enters your melee threat | Make a single [[Attacks|attack]] against them as they enter |
| Turn to Face | You need to rotate during Resolution | Spend your Reaction to change [[Facing|facing]] immediately |
| [[Movement Types|Movement]] Interrupt | You are currently moving during Resolution | Immediately change your [[Movement Types|movement]] direction, switch to a different target, or stop entirely at your current position |
| Use Cover | A ranged projectile is in flight toward you | Use your reaction and [[Movement Types|movement]] to get to cover. [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] is used to determine if the player gets there in time and normal [[Range & Threat|range]] rules apply. |

## [[Movement Types|Movement]] Reactions
During the Resolution Phase, if you are mid-[[Movement Types|movement]] you may spend your Reaction to:
- Change your [[Movement Types|movement]] direction entirely.
- Switch to a different destination or target.
- Stop at your current position along your declared path.

This represents a character reacting to unfolding events — ducking behind cover when an arrow is loosed, redirecting toward a new threat, or halting when a path becomes blocked. The Reaction resolves at the [[Speed & Resolution|Speed]] of your current [[Movement Types|movement]].

## Restrictions
You cannot use a Reaction if:
- You have already used a Reaction this [[Rounds|round]].
- You are Stunned, Immobilised, or otherwise prevented from acting.
