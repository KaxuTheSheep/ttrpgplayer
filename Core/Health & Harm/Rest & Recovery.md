Even the strongest characters cannot fight endlessly. Between encounters, rest allows the body and mind to recover from exhaustion, mend wounds, and regain lost vitality. Opportunities for genuine rest are precious and often determine who survives the next confrontation.

Rest comes in three forms, each offering different degrees of recovery depending on time, safety, and circumstance.

## Break (≈ 30 minutes)
A brief recovery during travel or downtime.
- Restore [[Sturdiness]] to full.
- You may spend 1 [[Blood]] Die to recover [[Blood]].
- You may spend 1 [[Sanity]] Die to recover [[Sanity]].
- Reduce Exhaustion by 1.
- Perform 1 Rest Activity.
- Minor Wounds do not heal, but may be treated.
- Attempt to reduce a Minor [[Afflictions|Affliction]]: Resolve Test (DN 16) → success reduces tier by 1.

> Breaks restore combat readiness, not health.

## Short Rest (≈ 2 hours)
Time to clean wounds, breathe, and recover enough to push forward.
- Restore [[Sturdiness]] to full.
- You may spend any number of [[Blood]] Dice to recover [[Blood]].
- You may spend any number of [[Sanity]] Dice to recover [[Sanity]].
- Attempt to close Open Wounds naturally: Vitality Test (DN 14) → on success, the wound becomes Closed.
- Perform up to 4 Rest Activities.
- Reduce Exhaustion by 1d4.

> Short Rests stabilise wounds — they do not remove them.

## Long Rest (≈ 2 weeks of safety)
Rare. Requires security, shelter, and absence of life-threatening stress.
- Fully restore [[Blood]], [[Sturdiness]], and [[Sanity]].
- Remove all Open and Closed Wounds.
- Reset Exhaustion to 0.
- Regain all [[Blood]] Dice and [[Sanity]] Dice.
- Attempt to reduce any [[Afflictions|Affliction]] (Minor or Moderate): Resolve Test (DN 12) → on success, reduce tier by 1.

> Permanent Injuries remain until treated by advanced means as defined by the relevant module.

## Blood Dice & Sanity Dice
Blood Dice and Sanity Dice start at **d4**. They represent your body's and mind's natural capacity to recover during rest. Spent dice are regained on a Long Rest. You gain 1 die every level, settings define how the die value increases.

## Rest Activities (≈ 30 minutes each)

| Activity | Effect |
| -------- | ------ |
| Catch Breath | Spend 1 Blood Die to restore Blood. |
| Patch Wounds | Medicine Test (DN 14): close 1 Open Wound on success. |
| Sharpen Focus | Gain advantage on 1 test of your choice. |
| Keep Watch | You and one ally gain Advantage on Perception during the rest. |
| Cook / Eat | Restore Sturdiness to full. |
| Repair Gear | Remove minor weapon or armour wear. |
| Meditate / Reflect | Reduce Exhaustion by 1. |
| Study / Research | Learn information via relevant Skill Test. |
