Weapons define how a character can fight in combat. Each weapon provides its own balance of reach, [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]], power, and defensive interaction. Choosing a weapon is choosing a fighting style.

All weapons share the following properties:

**[[Damage Types|Damage]] Die:** The base [[Damage Types|damage]] the weapon inflicts before adding [[Attributes|Attribute]].

**[[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] (Base):** Determines how quickly the weapon can be brought to bear. Higher [[Ticks, Priority & Resolution|Priority]] resolves earlier in the [[Rounds|round]].

**[[Range & Threat|Range]]:** How far from your body the weapon threatens space. Reach is listed as a **+X meters** value added to your natural melee [[Range & Threat|range]].

**Handedness:** One-Handed, Two-Handed, or Versatile.

**Weight Class:** Light, Standard, or Heavy.

**Traits:** Additional rules that modify how the weapon interacts with combat.
