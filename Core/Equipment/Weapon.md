Weapons define how a character can fight in combat. Each weapon provides its own balance of reach, speed, power, and defensive interaction. Choosing a weapon is choosing a fighting style.

All weapons share the following properties:

**Damage Die:** The base damage the weapon inflicts before adding Attribute.

**Speed (Base):** Determines how quickly the weapon can be brought to bear. Higher Speed resolves earlier in the round.

**Range:** How far from your body the weapon threatens space. Reach is listed as a **+X squares** value added to your natural melee range.

**Handedness:** One-Handed, Two-Handed, or Versatile.

**Weight Class:** Light, Standard, or Heavy.

**Traits:** Additional rules that modify how the weapon interacts with combat.
